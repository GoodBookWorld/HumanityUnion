## Purpose of this Specification

This document defines the complete architecture of Humanity Union and serves as the authoritative entry point for all engineering documentation. It establishes the platform's vision, mission, principles, and scope, and references the detailed specifications that describe each domain of the system. All design decisions, implementation work, and downstream documentation volumes must align with this master specification.

## Project Vision

Humanity Union is a unified digital platform that connects people, organizations, and communities around shared humanitarian goals. It aims to make collaboration, coordination, and positive impact accessible, transparent, and scalable across borders and contexts.

## Project Mission

To design and deliver a secure, reliable, and inclusive platform that enables individuals and institutions to participate in humanitarian action, share resources, and build lasting solidarity through well-defined processes, trustworthy data, and thoughtful technology.

## Core Principles

1. **Human-centered design** — Prioritize dignity, accessibility, and clarity in every user interaction and system behavior.
2. **Transparency and accountability** — Make processes, decisions, and outcomes traceable to foster trust among participants and stakeholders.
3. **Security and privacy by design** — Protect personal data, enforce least-privilege access, and embed safeguards throughout the architecture.
4. **Interoperability and openness** — Favor standards-based integration and extensible interfaces so the platform can evolve with its ecosystem.
5. **Reliability and resilience** — Build for continuity of service, graceful degradation, and recoverability under real-world conditions.
6. **Inclusive participation** — Ensure the platform serves diverse languages, abilities, regions, and organizational models without exclusion.
7. **Evidence-based iteration** — Ground architectural and product decisions in measurable outcomes, feedback, and documented rationale.

## Project Scope

This specification covers the full Humanity Union platform: user-facing applications, backend services, APIs, data management, deployment, operations, and the documentation volumes that detail each layer. It defines what the platform is intended to achieve and how its major components relate; individual volumes describe requirements, interfaces, and implementation guidance in depth.
