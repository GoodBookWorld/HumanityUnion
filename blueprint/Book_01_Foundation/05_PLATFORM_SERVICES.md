# Humanity Union Platform Services

## Version 1.0

### The Living Service Architecture

---

# Purpose

Platform Services are the operational foundation of Humanity Union.

They transform the principles defined in the Constitution, Charter, Information Architecture, and Living Platform Blueprint into practical capabilities.

Every Platform Service exists to support people, strengthen cooperation, and enable meaningful civic participation.

Services are not isolated software modules.

They are living capabilities that continuously interact with one another.

---

# Architectural Principle

Every Platform Service must satisfy three conditions.

1. It provides a reusable capability across the platform.

2. It strengthens at least one other Platform Service.

3. It contributes to the development of Human Potential.

A service that satisfies only technical requirements is incomplete.

---

# Platform Service Architecture

```text
Human Potential

↓

Human Experience

↓

Platform Services

```

Platform Services provide capabilities.

Platform Engines provide intelligence.

# Service Interaction Principle

No service should operate independently.

Every service should enrich another service.

Knowledge improves Initiatives.

Initiatives create Impact.

Impact generates Reflection.

Reflection improves Human Potential.

Human Potential creates new Initiatives.

The ecosystem continuously learns.

---

# Service Design Rules

Every Platform Service must be:

Reusable

Independent

Extensible

Observable

Secure

Explainable

Human-centered

Ethically aligned

Every service should expose clear APIs while remaining independent of interface design.

---

# Final Principle

Platform Services are not created to operate software.

They are created to strengthen people.

Every service should help at least one person:

understand better,

cooperate better,

or contribute more meaningfully.

If a service does not strengthen human potential, it does not belong in Humanity Union.
