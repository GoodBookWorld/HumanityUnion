# ENGINEERING_FOUNDATION_REVIEW

## Humanity Union Platform

Engineering Foundation Review

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This review verifies that the Humanity Union engineering foundation is complete, internally consistent, and ready to support long-term platform development.

The review evaluates engineering methodology rather than implementation.

# Success Criteria

Engineering Foundation is considered complete when:

- engineering methodology is documented;
- architectural governance is established;
- project continuity is ensured;
- documentation hierarchy is consistent;
- future Capabilities can follow the same lifecycle.
