# PLATFORM_ARCHITECTURE_REVIEW_V1

## Humanity Union Platform

Version 1.0

Platform Architecture Review

---

# Purpose

Perform the first complete architectural review of the Humanity Union Platform.

This review evaluates the platform as an integrated system rather than as individual Foundations, Capabilities or Epics.

Its objective is to determine whether the platform architecture is sufficiently stable for long-term evolution.

---

# Review Scope

The review includes:

Engineering Foundation

Collective Intelligence Foundation

Capability 01 — Human Identity

Capability 02 — Participation (Epic 01)

Platform command structure

Architecture governance

Repository organization
