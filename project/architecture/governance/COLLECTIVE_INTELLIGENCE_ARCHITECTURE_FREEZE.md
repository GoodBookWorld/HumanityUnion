# Responsibilities

Collective Intelligence is responsible for:

- reasoning support
- structured collaboration
- knowledge integration
- community memory
- insight generation
- reusable intelligence services

Capabilities remain responsible for:

- business rules
- workflows
- governance
- permissions
- execution

---

# Architecture Constraints

The platform shall never:

- replace human judgment;
- automate governance decisions;
- hide recommendation reasoning;
- destroy validated knowledge;
- force recommendations.

---

# Future Architecture

Future work may extend:

- Humanity Intelligence Assistant
- Collaborative Analysis
- Signal Engine
- Contribution Engine
- Progress Policy
- Knowledge Graph
- Recommendation Services

Extensions must preserve this architecture.

---

# Success Criteria

Collective Intelligence Foundation is considered stable when:

- terminology remains consistent;
- responsibilities remain separated;
- principles remain intact;
- implementation follows architecture;
- human leadership is preserved.

---

# Final Principle

Collective Intelligence exists to strengthen humanity's ability to think together.

It never replaces humanity's responsibility to decide together.
