# KNOWLEDGE_LIFECYCLE

## Humanity Union Platform

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

Define how knowledge is created, validated, preserved, reused and evolved throughout the Humanity Union Platform.

Knowledge is the primary long-term asset of Humanity Union.

Every Capability contributes to the growth of Community Memory.

---

# Mission

Transform individual human actions into collective knowledge that improves future decisions.

Knowledge is never created for a single Initiative.

It becomes part of Humanity Union's evolving Community Memory.

---

# Definition

Knowledge Lifecycle defines the complete journey of information inside Humanity Union.

Every meaningful Contribution follows this lifecycle.

---

---

# Stage 01 — Human Action

Every process begins with a person.

Actions include:

- creating an Initiative
- submitting Evidence
- asking a Question
- proposing an Alternative
- publishing a Reference
- adding an Expert Opinion
- improving an existing Initiative

Actions alone are not knowledge.

---

# Stage 02 — Contribution

A human action becomes a structured Contribution.

Contribution types include:

- Signal
- Comment
- Evidence
- Question
- Alternative
- Reference
- Expert Opinion
- Summary
- Correction

Every Contribution has:

- author
- purpose
- context
- timestamp
- relation to Initiative

---

# Stage 03 — Validation

Knowledge must be evaluated before becoming Community Memory.

Validation may include:

- community review
- expert review
- factual verification
- duplicate detection
- conflict identification
- completeness analysis

Validation strengthens trust.

---

# Stage 04 — Knowledge

Validated Contributions become reusable Knowledge.

Knowledge is independent from the original discussion.

Knowledge should remain useful beyond a single Initiative.

---

# Stage 05 — Community Memory

Community Memory stores validated knowledge.

Community Memory continuously grows.

It connects:

- Initiatives
- Evidence
- References
- Organizations
- Experts
- Communities
- Topics
- Regions

Knowledge is preserved rather than forgotten.

---

# Stage 06 — Collective Reasoning

Collective Reasoning combines accumulated knowledge to support future work.

Collective Reasoning:

- discovers relationships
- identifies patterns
- finds relevant experience
- highlights alternatives
- improves understanding

It never replaces human reasoning.

---

# Stage 07 — Decision Support

The platform provides:

- recommendations
- related initiatives
- evidence
- experts
- possible collaborations
- readiness evaluation

Recommendations remain optional.

Humans decide.

---

# Stage 08 — Better Decisions

Higher-quality knowledge leads to higher-quality decisions.

Decision quality increases through:

- transparency
- evidence
- collaboration
- structured analysis
- accumulated experience

---

# Stage 09 — Better Society

The final objective of Humanity Union is not information management.

It is societal improvement through better collective decision-making.

---

# Knowledge Evolution

Knowledge is never static.

Every validated Contribution may:

- expand existing knowledge
- refine previous knowledge
- connect multiple ideas
- resolve contradictions
- improve future recommendations

Community Memory evolves continuously.

---

# Relationship to Collective Intelligence

Knowledge Lifecycle provides the foundation for:

- Humanity Intelligence Layer
- Collaborative Analysis
- Initiative Assistant
- Community Memory
- Recommendation Engine
- Progress Evaluation
- Future Capabilities

---

# Success Criteria

Knowledge Lifecycle succeeds when:

- individual work benefits future participants;
- knowledge survives beyond a single Initiative;
- Community Memory continuously expands;
- Collective Reasoning becomes stronger over time;
- people make better decisions without losing ownership of those decisions.

---

# Final Principle

The greatest value created by Humanity Union is not information.

It is knowledge that continuously improves humanity's ability to make better decisions together.
