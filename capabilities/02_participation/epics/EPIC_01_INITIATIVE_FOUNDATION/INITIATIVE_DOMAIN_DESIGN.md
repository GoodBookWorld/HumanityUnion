# INITIATIVE_DOMAIN_DESIGN

## Capability 02 — Participation

## Epic 01 — Initiative Foundation

Humanity Union Platform

Domain Design

Version 1.0

---

# Vision

Every meaningful social improvement begins with an idea.

Humanity Union transforms ideas into structured public initiatives that evolve through constructive participation.

An Initiative is not a document.

An Initiative is a living social process.

---

# Mission

Provide a stable domain model that supports the complete lifecycle of a public initiative while preserving transparency, collaboration, historical continuity, and community stewardship.

---

# Core Domain

The core entity of Capability 02 is:

Initiative

All participation mechanisms operate around one Initiative.

Proposal, Discussion, Poll, Petition, and Implementation are lifecycle phases rather than independent domains.

---

# Initiative Identity

Every Initiative possesses a permanent identity.

Identity never changes during its lifecycle.

The Initiative may evolve through revisions, discussions, polls, and implementation, while remaining the same Initiative.

---

# Initiative Lifecycle

Initial lifecycle:

Draft

↓

Proposal

↓

Discussion

↓

Revision

↓

Ready for Poll

↓

Poll

↓

Petition

↓

Implementation

↓

Completed

Future states may include:

- Archived
- Revived
- Superseded
- Merged

Lifecycle extensions must preserve Initiative identity.

---

# Initiative Stewardship

Every Initiative has a Steward.

Initially, the Steward is the Initiative creator.

The Steward is responsible for maintaining the Initiative throughout its lifecycle.

Community participation never removes Initiative authorship.

Community participation may gradually expand Initiative governance.

---

# Contribution Model

Members contribute through constructive actions.

Examples:

- creating
- improving
- discussing
- reviewing
- voting
- implementing
- reviving

Every constructive contribution becomes part of Initiative history.

---

# Revision Model

Revision does not replace Initiative.

Revision improves Initiative.

Historical revisions remain preserved.

Every revision contributes to transparency.

---

# Timeline

Every Initiative owns one continuous Timeline.

The Timeline records:

- creation
- revisions
- discussions
- polls
- petitions
- implementation milestones
- completion

Timeline becomes permanent historical evidence.

---

# Ownership

Ownership and stewardship are related but distinct concepts.

Ownership identifies Initiative origin.

Stewardship governs Initiative evolution.

Future governance models may introduce shared stewardship while preserving original authorship.

---

# Visibility

Visibility belongs to Participation policy.

Visibility never changes Initiative identity.

Future Visibility & Consent capability may refine participation visibility.

---

# Domain Boundaries

Initiative owns:

- lifecycle
- revisions
- timeline
- stewardship

Initiative does not own:

- Member identity
- Fair
- Organizations
- Elections
- Funding
- Governance

---

# Future Extensions

Future capabilities may introduce:

- Initiative revival
- Initiative merging
- Initiative branching
- Cross-community initiatives
- International initiatives
- Evidence packages
- Decision tracking

These extensions must preserve Initiative continuity.

---

# Engineering Principles

This domain follows:

- Domain First
- Domain Ownership
- Progressive Bootstrap
- Initiative Continuity
- Initiative Stewardship
- Living Initiatives
- Continuous Contribution
- Constructive Participation

---

# Final Principle

Ideas become progress only when society can preserve, improve, and implement them together.
