# DOMAIN_LANGUAGE

## Capability 02 — Participation

## Epic 01 — Initiative Foundation

Humanity Union Platform

Domain Language

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the official vocabulary of the Participation domain.

Every architectural document, implementation guide, API, database schema, UI, and discussion should use these terms consistently.

No synonyms should be introduced for official domain concepts.

---

# Core Terms

## Initiative

The central entity representing a continuous public effort to improve society.

An Initiative preserves its identity throughout its lifecycle.

---

## Steward

The Member responsible for guiding an Initiative.

Initially the Initiative creator.

Stewardship may evolve while preserving original authorship.

---

## Contributor

A Member who makes constructive contributions to an Initiative.

---

## Contribution

Any constructive action that advances an Initiative.

Examples include:

- creation
- revision
- discussion
- review
- voting
- implementation
- revival

---

## Revision

A new version of an Initiative.

A Revision improves an Initiative without replacing its identity.

---

## Timeline

The complete chronological history of an Initiative.

The Timeline is permanent.

---

## Lifecycle

The ordered sequence of Initiative states.

Lifecycle describes Initiative evolution.

---

## Proposal

The first public presentation of an Initiative.

---

## Discussion

Collaborative improvement of an Initiative.

---

## Poll

Community measurement of support.

A Poll is exploratory.

---

## Petition

A formal request for action based on demonstrated community support.

---

## Implementation

The execution phase of an Initiative.

---

## Revival

Returning an archived Initiative to active development.

---

## Archive

Historical preservation of an inactive Initiative.

Archive never destroys Initiative history.

---

## Supersede

Replacement of one Initiative by another while preserving historical relationships.

---

## Living Initiative

An Initiative that continues evolving through constructive participation.

---

## Stewardship

Responsible guidance of an Initiative throughout its lifecycle.

Stewardship differs from ownership.

---

## Ownership

Original authorship of an Initiative.

Ownership is permanent.

---

## Participation

Constructive involvement of Members in Initiative evolution.

---

# Naming Rules

Always use official terms.

Avoid introducing alternate terminology.

Maintain identical terminology in:

- documentation
- architecture
- code
- APIs
- UI
- discussions

---

# Final Principle

Shared language creates shared understanding.

Shared understanding creates better systems.
