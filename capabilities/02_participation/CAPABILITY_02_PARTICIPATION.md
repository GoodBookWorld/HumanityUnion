# CAPABILITY_02_PARTICIPATION

## Humanity Union Platform

Capability Specification

Version 1.0

---

# Vision

Participation transforms Members into active contributors to society.

Humanity Union is not designed merely to collect opinions.

Its purpose is to help people develop ideas, improve them collaboratively, build public support, and turn them into real-world implementation.

Participation is therefore understood as a continuous social process rather than a collection of isolated tools.

---

# Mission

Enable every Member to participate constructively in improving society.

Participation must encourage:

- responsibility;
- collaboration;
- evidence-based discussion;
- transparency;
- long-term civic engagement.

---

# Core Domain

The central domain of this capability is:

Initiative

Everything else is built around the Initiative lifecycle.

Proposal, Discussion, Poll, Petition, and Implementation are not independent systems.

They are different phases of one Initiative.

---

# Initiative Lifecycle

Every Initiative evolves through a continuous lifecycle.

Initial lifecycle:

Draft

↓

Proposal

↓

Discussion

↓

Revision

↓

Ready for Poll

↓

Poll

↓

Petition

↓

Implementation

↓

Completed

Future lifecycle extensions may introduce:

- Archived
- Revived
- Superseded
- Merged

without changing the Initiative identity.

---

# Engineering Principles

Capability 02 follows all Humanity Union engineering principles.

Additionally it introduces:

## Principle of Constructive Participation

Participation mechanisms help Members build solutions together.

---

## Principle of Initiative Continuity

An Initiative remains one continuous entity throughout its lifecycle.

---

## Principle of Continuous Contribution

Every constructive contribution has value regardless of Initiative stage.

---

## Principle of Initiative Stewardship

The Initiative creator remains its steward while respecting community history.

---

## Principle of Living Initiatives

Initiatives evolve through collaboration.

Constructive ideas are preserved rather than discarded.

---

# Core Goals

Participation should:

- preserve ideas;
- improve ideas;
- measure constructive participation;
- encourage cooperation;
- support transparent decision-making;
- maintain historical continuity.
