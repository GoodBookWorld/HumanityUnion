# Purpose

Define the **architectural vision** of **Country Experience** within Humanity Union Public Space.

Country Experience presents **one country's civic activity** while preserving Humanity Union's **unified global perspective**.

It is Geographic Experience at **Country scope** — the national expression of the same public square Epic 02 established at World scope.

This document records **architectural intent** before content architecture, interaction architecture, specification and implementation.

It does not define implementation.

It does not authorize visual design, routing, components or Capability 02 engineering.

# 1. Vision

Country Experience allows people to **observe one country's public civic activity** within Humanity Union.

A visitor who narrows from the World public square to a country does not leave Humanity Union.

They **focus** — applying the same architectural lens to civic activity associated with one national place.

Country Experience strengthens **local understanding** without separating the country from Humanity Union's global civic space.

The national view answers a human question:

**"What is happening here — in this country — within structured civic participation?"**

Country Experience is not a national portal competing with Humanity Union.

It is **one country's window** into the same living society the Global Experience reveals at World scope.

Visitors may:

- observe national civic activity in public view;
- understand how participation is distributed within the country;
- explore regions, initiatives and related public records;
- return to World scope without disorientation;
- choose participation voluntarily — never as a condition of reading.

Country Experience presents **filtered public projections** — Capability 02 civic truth at country scope, composed by Capability 03 into the same Experience Blocks visitors already know from Global Experience.

It reveals national observable civic reality.

It does not promote a country, a government or a political narrative.

---

# 2. Purpose

Country Experience exists to:

## Present national civic participation

Make structured civic activity **visible at country scope** — initiatives, pipeline stages and geographic distribution within national boundaries as defined by public-safe metadata.

## Make national activity understandable

Help visitors interpret **what public records mean for this country** — through Context Introduction before Evidence, derived labeling and honest sparse states.

## Encourage local awareness

Support visitors who seek **place-relevant civic context** — residents, diaspora, researchers, journalists and curious global citizens — without trapping them in isolation.

## Preserve global context

Maintain **ascending paths to World scope**, unified header navigation and language that presents the country as a **participating place in one connected civic space** — not a separate platform.

**Country Experience explains rather than promotes.**

It orients nationally.

It never substitutes marketing for evidence.

It never demands registration to read public information.

Purpose aligns with Epic 01 Public Space model:

**Public Space enables observation.**

**Workspace enables participation.**

Country Experience remains firmly in Public Space.

---

# 3. Guiding Principle

**One Humanity.**

**Many Countries.**

**Shared Future.**

Countries remain **distinct** — named, locatable, culturally and geographically real to visitors.

Countries simultaneously **participate in one connected civic space** — Humanity Union — where structured civic activity may be observed across places without architectural fragmentation.

| Layer              | Meaning                                                                            |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **One Humanity**   | One Public Space architecture, one trust model, one platform identity              |
| **Many Countries** | Scope filter and national Identity — not separate products                         |
| **Shared Future**  | Global perspective preserved — national view deepens understanding, not separation |

Geographic scope changes **what data is shown**.

It does not change **what Humanity Union is**.

Country Experience expresses the guiding principle architecturally:

```
Same template

+

Country scope parameter

=

National civic observation within unified Public Space
```

Forbidden vision:

- national silos with different navigation;
- country pages that hide World context;
- rhetoric that pits countries against Humanity Union as institution.

# 4. Visitor Experience

Visitors to Country Experience should understand — naturally, through composition and calm interaction — the following:

## Where they are

**In Humanity Union, observing one country.**

Answered by: Hero Identity, Geographic Navigator with Country active, scope labels on Evidence blocks, reversible ascent to World.

## How this country contributes

**What public civic activity exists at national scope** — scale, pipeline shape and concrete initiative examples.

Answered by: Statistics, Participation Pipeline, Latest Initiatives, Interactive Map at country filter.

## How it relates to Humanity Union

**This country is part of one global public square** — not an exit from it.

Answered by: Context Introduction copy, World navigation entry, consistent global chrome, absence of national conversion pressure.

## How to explore deeper

**Where to go next** — regions within the country, initiative public detail, header destinations, related public records.

Answered by: map and navigator Region entry, initiative cards, Exploration links, header primary navigation.

## How to participate voluntarily

**Registration is optional** — available after sufficient national context, never required to read.

Answered by: Registration Gateway after Evidence sequence — same ethics as Global Experience.

Visitor experience preserves Epic 01 journey:

Discover → Understand → Trust → Register → Participate.

Stopping at Understand or Trust is valid.

Country scope **narrows context**.

It does not **accelerate registration**.

# 5. National Perspective

Country Experience introduces a **national perspective** through filtered public representations — not independent systems.

Each element below uses the **same architectural block** as Global Experience.

Only scope and Identity copy adapt.

## National identity

**Architectural role:** Identity layer — Hero and navigator name the country; optional restrained national visual context such as flag where policy permits.

**Vision constraint:** Identity orients — it does not certify political authority or rank countries.

National identity answers **where am I** — not **this country is superior**.

## National statistics

**Architectural role:** Statistics block Evidence — Participation Public Statistics Projection filtered to country scope.

**Vision constraint:** derived values labeled; honest zero or low activity; no league tables or engagement scores.

National statistics quantify **observable public activity** — not national worth.

## National initiatives

**Architectural role:** Latest Initiatives Evidence — Initiative Public Projection entries associated with the country.

**Vision constraint:** read-only cards linking to public detail; no operational fields; recency or curation transparent if curated.

National initiatives show **concrete civic paths** — not propaganda campaigns.

## Trusted national media

**Architectural role:** optional Exploration or secondary Evidence when Verified Media capability supplies country-associated public communication — never primary substitute for Participation projections.

**Vision constraint:** media supports context and verification — not authoritative participation records.

Trusted national media **documents public communication** — it does not replace civic Evidence.

## Regional exploration

**Architectural role:** Exploration layer — Interactive Map and Geographic Navigator entry to Region scope within the same country.

**Vision constraint:** region transition explains scope change; reversible; no Region pages required in Epic 03 vision — only architectural preparation.

Regional exploration **deepens local understanding** — it does not fork architecture.

**All national perspective elements are filtered public representations** composed into frozen Experience Blocks.

They are not separate national databases, national admin consoles or national product modules exposed in Public Space.

# 6. Relationship with Global Experience

Country Experience **inherits** the Global Experience architecture completely.

Epic 02 Global Experience is the **reference implementation** of Home-class composition at World scope.

Country Experience is Home-class composition at **Country scope**.

| Inherited from Global Experience | Unchanged                                                                                        |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Canonical block sequence         | Hero through Footer                                                                              |
| Global chrome                    | Header, Geographic Navigator, Footer                                                             |
| Page template flow               | Identity → Context Introduction → Evidence → Exploration → Participation → Supporting Navigation |
| Interaction philosophy           | Calm, voluntary, explainable, reversible                                                         |
| Trust model                      | Projection boundary, derived labeling, honest sparsity                                           |
| Registration ethics              | Observation precedes participation                                                               |
| Primary navigation               | Six frozen header destinations                                                                   |

**Only the geographic scope changes.**

World datasets become country-filtered datasets.

World Identity copy becomes country Identity copy.

World map Evidence becomes country-regional Evidence.

Interaction model, trust model and architectural language remain **identical**.

```
Global Experience

+ country scope

= Country Experience
```

Visitors descend from World — they do not migrate to a different product.

---

# 7. Relationship with Region Experience

Country Experience **prepares visitors for regional exploration** within the same country.

Region Experience — future Epic at Region scope — inherits the **same architecture** again.

```
World     → Global Experience
Country   → Country Experience    ← this Epic
Region    → Region Experience     → future Epic
```

| Transition           | Visitor understanding                       |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **World → Country**  | Global civic activity narrows to one nation |
| **Country → Region** | National activity narrows to one region     |
| **Region → Country** | Ascent restores national context            |
| **Country → World**  | Ascent restores global public square        |

Country Experience must:

- expose Region as explicit navigator and map exploration entry where public data supports it;
- explain scope change when Region is selected — same interaction discipline as World → Country;
- preserve primary header destinations and template sequence at Region scope — architecturally, not in Epic 03 build scope.

Country is the **parent scope** for Region in the frozen hierarchy.

Country Experience establishes national orientation **regional exploration completes**.

# 8. Architectural Principles

Country Experience conforms to frozen Epic 01 and Epic 02 principles without exception.

## Public Space is the window into a living society

Country pages present **observable national civic activity** — not national branding exercises.

## Observation precedes participation

Registration Gateway follows national Evidence — never opens the country page.

## Context Before Evidence

Every block at country scope: **Heading → Context Introduction → Evidence**.

National Context Introduction explains significance — never persuades.

## Trust Through Verification

Visitors trace national Evidence to initiative public detail and derived labels — same verification path as Global Experience.

## Explainable Honesty

National sparse or zero activity shown honestly — not inflated to appear nationally "active."

## One Experience Block — One Responsibility

Country Experience uses the same block catalog — no national megablocks merging Identity, Evidence and Registration.

## Filter Instead of Duplicate

Country Experience is **scope filter on unified architecture** — not CountryPageTemplate, not parallel block library, not country-specific navigation logic.

Principles govern tone and honesty.

Template governs sequence.

Scope governs datasets.

Country Experience adds **national focus** — not **national exception**.

# 9. Success Criteria

Country Experience vision succeeds when a visitor moving from Global Experience to Country Experience **immediately recognizes**:

| Recognition                             | Expected outcome                                                     |
| --------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **The same Humanity Union**             | Global chrome, platform identity, connected civic space language     |
| **The same architectural language**     | Block order, page rhythm, Context Before Evidence                    |
| **The same interaction philosophy**     | Calm scope change, reversible navigation, no pressure                |
| **National scope naturally understood** | "I am observing this country's civic activity within Humanity Union" |

Success within approximately **thirty seconds** at Country scope means:

- the country is identified clearly;
- national public activity is legible or honestly sparse;
- World return path is obvious;
- registration is not demanded.

Success includes visitors who:

- ascend to World without relearning navigation;
- explore initiatives nationally without an account;
- proceed toward Region when available;
- leave successfully without registering.

Failure modes to avoid:

- national page feels like a different website;
- header or block order changes at country scope;
- national rhetoric replaces Evidence;
- hidden or broken World ascent.

---

# 10. Final Statement

**Country Experience is the national expression of Humanity Union Public Space.**

It preserves **one architecture**, **one philosophy**, and **one public trust model** while presenting **one country's observable civic reality**.

Country Experience does not fragment Humanity Union.

It **focuses** Humanity Union — one country at a time — for visitors who need national civic context within the same public institution Global Experience introduced at World scope.

Downstream artifacts — content architecture, interaction architecture, narrative, page specification, architecture review and implementation plan — must inherit this vision and conform to frozen Epic 01 and Epic 02 documents.

**This document does not define implementation.**

It defines **what Country Experience must mean** architecturally before build authorization.

One Humanity.

Many Countries.

Shared Future.

One Public Space — observed globally, understood nationally, explored regionally.
