# 1. Purpose

Define the **interaction architecture** of Country Experience.

This document explains how visitors **progressively explore one country's civic activity** while preserving Humanity Union's unified Public Space architecture.

Country Experience **guides visitors through understanding** one country's observable civic reality — not through directed funnels, national pressure or registration optimization.

**Every interaction increases understanding rather than encouraging immediate participation.**

Interactions are architectural commitments about:

- what the visitor may do at country scope;
- what changes when they do it;
- what they learn from the change;
- how they continue, ascend or return.

This document does not define UI implementation, animations, frontend behaviour, components or visual design.

# 2. Interaction Philosophy

Country Experience interactions inherit Global Experience philosophy without national exception.

Interactions should be:

| Quality         | Architectural meaning at country scope                                                   |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Voluntary**   | Visitor chooses to act — no forced national paths, gates or interruptive demands         |
| **Predictable** | Same interaction class produces same outcome — World, Country and Region share behaviour |
| **Reversible**  | Visitor returns to World, prior country view or region without penalty                   |
| **Explainable** | Visitor understands scope change and national context before or immediately after acting |
| **Respectful**  | Interactions honour attention, dignity and autonomy — no nationalist dark patterns       |
| **Calm**        | No urgency, streaks, countdowns or moral pressure tied to country identity               |

**Every interaction reduces uncertainty rather than creating it.**

After interacting, the visitor should know:

- where they are — country named, Humanity Union context preserved;
- what changed — scope filter, detail navigation or Evidence refresh;
- what they learned — national or regional civic meaning;
- how to continue — initiatives, regions, header destinations;
- how to go back — World ascent, country reset, browser back, navigator.

Forbidden interaction philosophy at country scope:

- national registration conversion optimization;
- surprise scope changes that feel like leaving Humanity Union;
- hiding national public Evidence until account creation;
- interactions that rank or shame countries;
- decorative clicks without learning outcome.

Interactions serve **national public understanding within global architecture** — not platform metrics alone.

# 3. Learning Path

The canonical **exploration sequence** for Country Experience interactions:

```
Observe

↓

Understand the Country

↓

Explore National Activity

↓

Explore Regions

↓

Evaluate

↓

Participate (optional)
```

## Observe

Visitor encounters Country Experience — Country Identity, national map, first scan.

**Interaction goal:** notice **which country** is active and that national civic activity is observable in Humanity Union.

No commitment required.

## Understand the Country

Visitor reads national Context Introductions and Evidence — statistics, pipeline, initiative previews.

**Interaction goal:** grasp **structure and scale** of public civic activity **in this country**.

Comprehension deepens nationally — registration does not.

## Explore National Activity

Visitor acts — initiative card links, header destinations, national map inspection, related public records.

**Interaction goal:** move from national aggregates to **concrete civic subjects** and supporting context.

Exploration remains **within country scope** unless visitor deliberately changes scope.

## Explore Regions

Visitor narrows geographic filter to **Region scope** within the same country — map or Geographic Navigator.

**Interaction goal:** understand that civic activity can be observed **more locally** with **same architecture**.

Understanding becomes **progressively more local** — not a different product.

## Evaluate

Visitor inspects initiative public detail, derived labels, trusted media cross-checks, About paths.

**Interaction goal:** judge legitimacy through **verification** — not national rhetoric.

Trust builds through **exploration and traceability**.

## Participate (optional)

Visitor chooses Registration Gateway — optional transition to registration and Workspace.

**Interaction goal:** informed entry to accountable participation **after national observation**.

**Understanding becomes progressively more local** across the path:

```
Humanity Union (global institution)

↓

Country (national filter)

↓

Region (local filter within country)

↓

Initiative detail (named civic path)

↓

Optional Workspace (accountable action)
```

Participate is valid.

Stopping at Evaluate is valid.

Skipping to Participate without Observe–Evaluate is architecturally discouraged — not forbidden by account gate, but not designed for.

# 4. Country Experience Interaction Flow

End-to-end interaction flow from country entry through optional participation:

```
Visitor

↓

Country Experience

↓

National Exploration

↓

Regional Exploration

↓

Public Understanding

↓

Registration (optional)

↓

Workspace
```

## Visitor

External entry — from Global Experience descent, shared country URL, search or navigator.

**Transition responsibility:** land in **unified public architecture** at Country scope — not operational workspace, not national portal fork.

## Country Experience

World-filter narrows to **one country**.

**Transition responsibility:** orient nationally — Country Identity, Context Introduction, national Evidence sequence.

Visitor understands they remain **inside Humanity Union**.

## National Exploration

Visitor interacts with national map, statistics, pipeline, initiatives, trusted media.

**Transition responsibility:** deepen **national** comprehension — aggregates to examples to detail links.

Scope remains **Country** unless visitor explicitly changes it.

## Regional Exploration

Visitor selects **Region** within country — map or Geographic Navigator.

**Transition responsibility:** apply **finer geographic filter** — Identity and Evidence refresh at Region scope when Region Experience exists.

Same template — narrower dataset.

## Public Understanding

Visitor reaches sufficient comprehension — national structure, concrete initiatives, optional regional context, derived labels understood.

**Transition responsibility:** support **evaluation** without platform conclusion.

Trust through verification — not slogans.

## Registration (optional)

Visitor chooses Registration Gateway.

**Transition responsibility:** offer **informed** Identity entry — never required for reading.

## Workspace

Governed operational participation — Capability 02 workspaces.

**Transition responsibility:** accountable civic action **after** public understanding.

Country Experience interaction flow **extends** Global Experience flow — it does not replace it.

Ascent to **Global Experience** remains available at every stage.

# 5. Geographic Exploration

Geographic exploration at and below Country scope follows frozen hierarchy:

```
Country

↓

Region

↓

Future Local Community Experience

↓

Workspace
```

Above Country, **World** remains the global ascent target:

```
World

↓

Country   ← this Epic

↓

Region

↓

Future Local Community Experience
```

Future local levels may include District, Municipality, City, Village, Neighbourhood, Indigenous Territory and additional community structures — architecture **must not assume fixed depth**.

## Interaction model

| Transition                       | What changes                                                                    | What does not change                                                    |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **World → Country**              | Dataset scope; Identity framing; statistics and initiatives filtered nationally | Page template; block types; header destinations; interaction philosophy |
| **Country → Region**             | Finer geographic filter within same country                                     | Architecture language; primary navigation; block sequence               |
| **Region → Country**             | Broader national filter restore                                                 | Template and navigation                                                 |
| **Country → World**              | Global filter restore                                                           | Unified Public Space model                                              |
| **Any public scope → Workspace** | Environment shift to operational participation                                  | Projection discipline on public pages                                   |

**Only the geographic scope changes.**

**Architecture remains identical.**

## Primary geographic interactions at Country scope

- **Geographic Navigator** — explicit Country active; Region entry; World ascent;
- **National Interactive Map** — regional drill-down entry within country;
- **Scoped URLs or state** — deep link to Country or Region Geographic Experience;
- **Regional Exploration block** — explains and supports scope transition honestly.

## Reversibility

Visitor returns to World or Country without losing navigation context.

Scope change must signal **what changed** — country label, scope parameter, filtered Evidence refresh.

Forbidden: scope transition that reloads different header set, hides primary destinations or feels like exiting Humanity Union.

# 6. Information Exploration

Information exploration **narrows geographic context while increasing understanding**.

```
Country Overview

↓

National Statistics

↓

Participation Pipeline

↓

National Initiatives

↓

Trusted National Media

↓

Regional Exploration

↓

Participation
```

## Country Overview

**Interaction locus:** Country Identity · first scan · Geographic Navigator confirmation.

**Understanding gain:** _Where am I nationally in Humanity Union?_

## National Statistics

**Interaction locus:** National Statistics block; optional expand or calm cross-link.

**Understanding gain:** _How much public civic activity is visible in this country?_

## Participation Pipeline

**Interaction locus:** National Participation Pipeline block.

**Understanding gain:** _How is participation distributed across stages nationally?_

## National Initiatives

**Interaction locus:** Latest National Initiatives cards; links to public initiative detail.

**Understanding gain:** _What concrete civic paths exist here?_

## Trusted National Media

**Interaction locus:** Trusted National Media block — optional.

**Understanding gain:** _What verified public communication supports this context?_

## Regional Exploration

**Interaction locus:** Map region selection · navigator Region entry · Regional Exploration guidance.

**Understanding gain:** _How do I observe more locally within this country?_

## Participation

**Interaction locus:** Registration Gateway.

**Understanding gain:** _What registration enables — chosen action._

Each step is **optional** — visitor may jump via header, deep link or Related Content — but **presentation order** on Country Experience follows this progression.

Narrowing context:

- national statistics → one initiative → one stage evidence → optional region;

Increasing understanding:

- abstract national counts → named civic path → verifiable public detail → optional local scope.

**Every interaction narrows geographic context while increasing understanding** — or ascends scope deliberately with same discipline.

# 7. Interaction Principles

The following principles are **frozen** for Country Experience and connected public interactions.

## Every interaction increases understanding

If an interaction cannot articulate what the visitor learned about **this country's public civic activity**, it should not exist.

## No dead ends

Every interaction outcome offers **continue, return or cross-link** — not blank terminus without orientation.

Country scope unavailable, sparse data or deferred Region routes must explain honestly — with World or Country return path.

## Visitors always know where they are

Country name, scope label, navigator state and Identity layer remain legible after interaction.

## Visitors always know what changed

Scope filter, initiative detail navigation and Evidence refresh **explicitly signal change** — not silent substitution.

## Visitors always know where to continue

Exploration links, header destinations, initiative cards, regional entry and Related Content visible from outcomes.

## Visitors always know how to return

World ascent, Country reset, breadcrumb or navigator ascent, header Home — **reversible paths preserved**.

## Navigation follows curiosity

No forced linear tutorial, locked national content or registration wall on public Evidence.

Forbidden at country scope:

- interruptive overlay before Context Introduction read path;
- national guilt framing on exit;
- bait-and-switch from public reading to operational workspace without explicit governed entry.

# 8. Context Before Evidence

Every interaction preserves the pattern:

```
Heading

↓

Context Introduction

↓

Evidence

↓

Visitor Conclusion
```

| Layer                    | Interaction role at country scope                              |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Heading**              | Visitor identifies national interaction subject                |
| **Context Introduction** | Visitor understands why national Evidence below matters        |
| **Evidence**             | Visitor receives country-filtered public facts and projections |
| **Visitor Conclusion**   | Visitor judges — platform does not conclude for them           |

Interactions that reveal new Evidence — scope change to Region, initiative detail navigation, media expand — must preserve **Context Introduction before new Evidence layer**.

**Humanity Union presents evidence.**

**Visitors form conclusions independently.**

Interactions must not insert platform verdicts, national certification copy or "trust this country" messaging at conclusion layer.

Registration Gateway is **invitation** — not platform conclusion about visitor judgment.

---

# 9. Trust Through Interaction

Country Experience builds trust through **interaction design discipline** — not through persuasion.

Trust grows when visitors can **verify** what they observe.

## Observable civic activity

National map, initiatives and pipeline interactions lead to **traceable public records** — not claims.

## Verified public statistics

Statistics interactions expose **derived labeling** and scope — visitor can question aggregates honestly presented.

## Public initiatives

Initiative card interactions open **public projection detail** — civic path readable without account.

## Trusted national media

Media interactions support **cross-check context** — media never overrides participation Evidence authority.

## Transparent navigation

Scope changes, placeholder states and return paths are **explainable** — no hidden national funnels.

**Trust grows through understanding rather than persuasion.**

Country Experience must not ask visitors to trust through:

- national rhetoric;
- flag-display authority;
- urgency or moral pressure;
- obscured sparse data.

Trust Through Verification applies at **country scope** identically to World scope.

# 10. Relationship with Global Experience

Country Experience **inherits the Global Experience interaction model** completely.

| Inherited interaction element | Country behaviour                                                                           |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Learning path shape           | Observe → Understand → Explore → Evaluate → Participate — with national and regional nuance |
| Interaction philosophy        | Voluntary, predictable, reversible, explainable, respectful, calm                           |
| Header navigation             | Same six destinations — same interaction outcomes                                           |
| Geographic Navigator pattern  | Scope control — Country active instead of World                                             |
| Map interaction class         | Scope exploration entry — national/regional instead of world/country                        |
| Initiative detail entry       | Same public projection pages                                                                |
| Registration Gateway ethics   | After Evidence — optional                                                                   |
| Context Before Evidence       | Mandatory on all blocks                                                                     |
| Reversibility                 | World ascent always available                                                               |

**Visitors should never need to relearn navigation** when descending from Global Experience to Country Experience.

Interaction muscle memory transfers:

- same header;
- same block rhythm;
- same calm scope-change semantics;
- same trust verification path.

Country is **narrower context** — not **new interaction language**.

# 11. Relationship with Region Experience

Country Experience **prepares visitors for Region Experience**.

| Country role                                   | Region continuation                             |
| ---------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| National orientation complete                  | Regional filter applied                         |
| Map and navigator expose Region entry          | Region page inherits same block sequence        |
| Regional Exploration block explains transition | Region Identity replaces Country Identity label |
| Reversible Country ascent                      | Region ↔ Country ↔ World preserved              |

**Region Experience continues the same interaction architecture.**

Region interactions do not introduce:

- new header destinations;
- new registration pressure;
- new block order;
- new trust model.

Country → Region transition interaction must:

- signal **what changed** — region name, finer filter;
- preserve **explainability** — same template language;
- avoid **dead ends** — honest stub if Region not yet implemented, with Country return.

Country Experience is the **parent interaction context** for regional depth within one nation.

# 12. Future Evolution

Future additions must **integrate without changing** Country Experience interaction architecture.

| Future addition                | Integration rule                                                       |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Local Community Experience** | Deeper geographic scope — same interaction classes                     |
| **Advanced Search**            | Exploration extension — does not replace block sequence                |
| **Bookmarks**                  | Visitor convenience — must not gate public Evidence                    |
| **AI Discovery Assistant**     | Explain-only — never decides, registers or certifies national activity |
| **Personal Collections**       | Optional visitor organization — must not gate public reading           |

Extension rules:

- canonical learning path preserved;
- Context Before Evidence preserved;
- Every interaction increases understanding preserved;
- No dead ends preserved;
- Observation precedes participation preserved;
- Filter Instead of Duplicate preserved.

New interaction types require **Architecture Review** and Block Library entry — not Epic 03 implementation convenience.

Future capabilities **extend exploration** — they do not **replace** Country Experience interaction model.

Content architecture governs block meaning.

Template governs sequence.

Country scope governs **filter** — not **exception**.

# 14. Final Statement

**Country Experience helps visitors understand one country's observable civic reality within Humanity Union's connected public space.**

Visitors explore nationally and regionally through **meaningful interactions** — voluntarily, predictably, reversibly and calmly.

**The interaction architecture remains stable** while **geographic understanding becomes progressively more local**.

Country Experience does not teach visitors a new platform.

It teaches them **more about one place** inside the platform they already learned at World scope.

Downstream artifacts — page specification, architecture review and implementation plan — must conform to this interaction architecture and frozen Epic 01 and Epic 02 documents.

**This document does not define UI implementation.**

**This document does not define animations.**

It defines **how Country Experience interactions must behave** architecturally — before engineering interprets them.

One Humanity.

Many Countries.

Shared Future.

One interaction model — observed globally, explored nationally, continued regionally.
