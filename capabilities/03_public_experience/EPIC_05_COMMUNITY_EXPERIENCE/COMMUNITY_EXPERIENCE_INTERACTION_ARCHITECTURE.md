# 1. Purpose

Define the **interaction architecture** of Community Experience.

This document explains how visitors **understand one participant-created community** and **naturally transition from public observation into personal participation through Workspace**.

Community Experience **guides visitors from understanding one community toward informed personal participation** — not through directed funnels, organizational pressure or registration optimization.

**Every interaction increases understanding before any action is invited.**

Interactions are architectural commitments about:

- what the visitor may do at community scope;
- what changes when they do it;
- what they learn from the change;
- how they continue toward initiative detail, other communities, broader scopes or Workspace.

This document does not define UI implementation, animations, frontend behaviour, components or visual design.

# 2. Interaction Philosophy

Community Experience interactions inherit Global, Country and Region Experience philosophy without community exception.

Interactions should be:

| Quality         | Architectural meaning at community scope                                                                 |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Voluntary**   | Visitor chooses to act — no forced community paths, gates or interruptive demands                        |
| **Predictable** | Same interaction class produces same outcome — World, Country, Region and Community share behaviour      |
| **Reversible**  | Visitor returns to Region, Country, World, prior community view or search without penalty                |
| **Explainable** | Visitor understands scope change, community context and civic meaning before or immediately after acting |
| **Respectful**  | Interactions honour attention, dignity and autonomy — no organizational boosterism or dark patterns      |
| **Calm**        | No urgency, streaks, countdowns or moral pressure tied to community identity                             |

**Every interaction reduces uncertainty rather than creating it.**

After interacting, the visitor should know:

- where they are — community name, broader scope context, Humanity Union preserved;
- what changed — community filter, initiative detail navigation, search results or Evidence refresh;
- what they learned — community civic meaning or cross-community discovery outcome;
- how to continue — initiatives, Find Your Community, header destinations, Workspace when ready;
- how to go back — Region ascent, Country ascent, World ascent, browser back, navigator.

Forbidden interaction philosophy at community scope:

- community registration conversion optimization;
- surprise scope changes that feel like leaving Humanity Union;
- hiding community public Evidence until account creation;
- interactions that rank or shame communities;
- decorative clicks without learning outcome;
- Find Your Community forced before public reading elsewhere on platform;
- collapsing Community scope into Workspace without explicit governed transition;
- organizational membership recruitment masquerading as civic observation.

Interactions serve **community public understanding and informed personal participation** — not platform metrics alone.

# 3. Learning Path

The canonical **exploration sequence** for Community Experience interactions:

```
Observe

↓

Understand the Community

↓

Explore Community Activity

↓

Evaluate Community Contribution

↓

Decide Personally

↓

Workspace
```

## Observe

Visitor encounters Community Experience — Community Identity, first scan, Geographic Navigator confirmation.

**Interaction goal:** notice **which participant-created community** is active and that community-scoped civic activity is observable in Humanity Union.

No commitment required.

## Understand the Community

Visitor reads community Context Introductions, Community Description and Identity framing.

**Interaction goal:** grasp **who this community is and why it exists** — participant-named civic context, not admin taxonomy.

Comprehension deepens at community scope — registration does not.

## Explore Community Activity

Visitor acts — Community Statistics, Participation Pipeline, initiative card links, header destinations.

**Interaction goal:** move from community Identity to **structured observable activity** — aggregates, stage distribution and concrete initiative examples.

Exploration remains **within community scope** unless visitor deliberately changes scope toward another community, ascends to Region or explores initiative detail.

## Evaluate Community Contribution

Visitor interacts with **Community Impact Overview** and verifies upstream Evidence — statistics, pipeline and initiatives.

**Interaction goal:** synthesize **observable public contribution** through visitor judgment — platform presents evidence; visitor evaluates impact.

Evaluation is **evidence-based** — not platform verdict.

## Decide Personally

Visitor chooses whether to continue observing, discover other communities through Find Your Community, ascend to broader scopes, or proceed toward Registration Gateway or Workspace continuation.

**Interaction goal:** **personal decision** after sufficient public context — no pressure, no default funnel.

Stopping at observation is valid.

## Workspace

Visitor enters governed **personal operational participation** — optional, after informed choice.

**Interaction goal:** accountable civic action **after** community public understanding.

**The visitor completes public observation before personal participation:**

```
Humanity Union (global institution)

↓

Country (national filter)

↓

Region (regional filter)

↓

Community (participant-named civic context)

↓

Initiative detail (named civic path)

↓

Workspace (personal accountable action — optional)
```

Understanding becomes **progressively more participant-local** — not a different product at each step.

Workspace entry is valid.

Stopping at Evaluate Community Contribution is valid.

Stopping at Understand the Community is valid.

Skipping to Workspace without Observe–Evaluate is architecturally discouraged — not forbidden by account gate, but not designed for.

# 4. Community Experience Flow

End-to-end interaction flow from community entry through optional Workspace:

```
Visitor

↓

Community Experience

↓

Community Exploration

↓

Community Initiatives

↓

Community Impact

↓

Workspace Transition
```

## Visitor

External entry — from Region Community Discovery, Find Your Community search, Initiative association link, shared community URL or navigator.

**Transition responsibility:** land in **unified public architecture** at Community scope — not operational Workspace, not community portal fork.

## Community Experience

Scope narrows to **one participant-created community**.

**Transition responsibility:** orient at community scope — Community Identity, Context Introduction, community Evidence sequence; Region and Country ascent preserved where metadata supports.

Visitor understands they remain **inside Humanity Union**.

## Community Exploration

Visitor interacts with Community Statistics and Community Participation Pipeline on Community page.

**Interaction locus:** quantitative and structural Evidence blocks.

**Transition responsibility:** deepen **community-scoped** comprehension — _how much_ and _at what stages_ public activity is observable.

Scope remains **Community** unless visitor explicitly transitions toward initiative detail, Find Your Community, broader scopes or Workspace.

**Note:** _Community Exploration_ here names **interaction with community Evidence blocks** — not a separate product surface.

## Community Initiatives

Visitor interacts with **Latest Community Initiatives** — initiative cards, links to public initiative detail.

**Transition responsibility:** move from structure to **concrete civic subjects** associated with the community — verifiable public paths.

Initiative detail remains **public projection pages** — readable without Workspace.

## Community Impact

Visitor interacts with **Community Impact Overview** — synthesis Evidence, verification links, Visitor Conclusion layer.

**Transition responsibility:** enable **visitor-led evaluation** of observable outcomes — no platform subjective scoring interaction.

Impact interaction **does not replace** reading Statistics, Pipeline or Initiatives — it **synthesizes** after them.

## Workspace Transition

Visitor chooses **Registration Gateway** (unauthenticated) or **Continue to Workspace** (authenticated) — calm, explicit boundary crossing.

**Transition responsibility:** offer **informed personal participation entry** — never required for reading.

Community Experience interaction flow **completes** Region Experience flow — it does not replace it.

Ascent to **Region**, **Country** and **Global Experience** remains available at every stage.

Deep link directly to Community Experience is valid — template preserved — not architecture failure.

Find Your Community may occur **before or after** Impact depending on page type — see Section 5 and content architecture discovery surface exception — but **Workspace Transition always follows sufficient community observation** on community observation pages.

# 5. Information Exploration

Information exploration **narrows civic context while increasing understanding of real civic participation**.

```
Community Identity

↓

Community Statistics

↓

Participation Pipeline

↓

Latest Community Initiatives

↓

Community Impact Overview

↓

Find Your Community

↓

Workspace
```

## Community Identity

**Interaction locus:** Community Identity · Community Name · Description · Activity Area · first scan · Geographic Navigator confirmation.

**Understanding gain:** _Who is this community in public civic space — and why does observation below matter?_

## Community Statistics

**Interaction locus:** Community Statistics block; optional calm cross-link to initiative detail or Knowledge.

**Understanding gain:** _How much public civic activity is observable around this community?_

## Participation Pipeline

**Interaction locus:** Community Participation Pipeline block.

**Understanding gain:** _How is participation distributed across stages at community scope?_

## Latest Community Initiatives

**Interaction locus:** Latest Community Initiatives cards; links to public initiative detail.

**Understanding gain:** _What concrete civic paths are associated with this community?_

## Community Impact Overview

**Interaction locus:** Community Impact Overview synthesis Evidence; verification links; Visitor Conclusion interaction.

**Understanding gain:** _What observable outcomes can I infer from public evidence — by my own judgment?_

## Find Your Community

**Interaction locus:** Find Your Community search; result selection; transition to another Community Experience.

**Understanding gain:** _How do I discover other participant-created communities — and how is search different from Region browse?_

On **discovery landing surfaces**, Find Your Community may precede Community Identity when no community is selected — search interaction still increases understanding of **participant-created discovery model**.

## Workspace

**Interaction locus:** Registration Gateway or Continue to Workspace.

**Understanding gain:** _What personal participation enables — chosen action after public observation._

Each step is **optional** — visitor may jump via header, deep link, search result or Related Content — but **presentation order** on Community Experience follows this progression.

Narrowing context:

- community statistics → one initiative → public initiative detail → optional Workspace;

Increasing understanding:

- abstract community counts → named civic path → verifiable public detail → personal decision.

**Every interaction increases understanding of real civic participation** — structured, projection-backed, voluntary — while preserving Humanity Union architecture.

Same header, same block rhythm, same trust model, same calm scope semantics.

# 6. Find Your Community

**Find Your Community principles are frozen** for Community Experience interactions — Version 1.

## Find Your Community helps visitors locate participant-created communities

Search interactions surface **Community records created through participation** — increasing understanding that communities are **participant-named civic context**, not administrator-imposed partitions.

Locating another community is **exploration** — not mandatory path completion.

## Find Your Community supports search

Search targets **Community Name** and **Community Description** — public-safe fields governed by Capability 02.

Search interactions must:

- return **explainable results** — including empty, sparse and duplicate-name states;
- signal **what changed** when visitor selects a result — community scope refresh;
- preserve **reversibility** — return to prior community or broader scopes without penalty;
- avoid **engagement-optimized ranking** without explainable honesty.

## Find Your Community does not classify communities through administrator-controlled taxonomies

No mandatory Community type taxonomy interaction in Version 1.

Activity Area may support **governed filter dimensions** — distinct from administrator catalog classification.

Search is **participant-record discovery** — not **platform civic sorting authority**.

## Find Your Community returns communities created through participation

Results reflect **public Community records** from civic participation — not exhaustive administrator-maintained directory presented as sole truth.

Empty results must be **honest** — no fabricated communities.

## Relationship with Region Community Discovery

| Interaction                         | Scope              | Behaviour                                       |
| ----------------------------------- | ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| **Community Discovery** (Region)    | Association browse | Prepares Community descent — links forward      |
| **Find Your Community** (Community) | Name search        | Primary search — Community Experience exclusive |

Region **browse** and Community **search** are complementary — neither replaces the other.

Find Your Community interactions must:

- never **force** community selection before public reading elsewhere on platform;
- never **duplicate** Region browse as embedded primary behaviour on community observation page without distinct responsibility;
- always offer **continue, return or cross-link** from search outcomes — no dead ends.

# 7. Workspace Transition

**Workspace becomes available only after visitors understand:**

| Prerequisite understanding  | Interaction source                                                                          |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Who the community is**    | Community Identity · Description · Context Introduction                                     |
| **What it contributes**     | Community Statistics · Pipeline · Latest Initiatives                                        |
| **How participation works** | Pipeline vocabulary · initiative detail links · calm Registration Gateway or Workspace copy |

Workspace Transition interactions appear **after** community Evidence weight and Find Your Community on canonical community observation pages — not before Community Identity on those pages.

## Unauthenticated visitor

**Registration Gateway interaction** — calm invitation after observation.

Visitor may **decline** and continue reading public Evidence — valid outcome.

## Authenticated participant

**Continue to Workspace interaction** — governed personal entry after observation.

Visitor may **decline** and continue public observation — valid outcome.

## Boundary discipline

| Rule                       | Application                                                                                             |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Voluntary**              | Participation remains voluntary — no registration wall on public Evidence                               |
| **Explicit**               | Workspace transition signals environment change — public observation → personal operation               |
| **Informed**               | Context Introduction precedes action — not bait-and-switch                                              |
| **Reversible**             | Visitor may return to public Community Experience from governed entry points where architecture permits |
| **No premature Workspace** | Operational workspace UI not embedded in Public Space blocks                                            |

Workspace is **not** a Community Experience block interaction class — it is **governed continuation** beyond Public Space.

Community Experience **completes public interaction layer**.

Workspace **begins personal interaction layer**.

# 8. Interaction Principles

The following principles are **frozen** for Community Experience and connected public interactions.

## Every interaction increases understanding

If an interaction cannot articulate what the visitor learned about **this community's public civic activity**, **cross-community discovery**, or **personal participation choice**, it should not exist.

## No dead ends

Every interaction outcome offers **continue, return or cross-link** — not blank terminus without orientation.

Community scope unavailable, sparse data, empty search results, empty initiative lists or deferred Workspace routes must explain honestly — with Region return path and header continue paths.

## Visitors always know where they are

Community Name, scope label, navigator state, Identity layer and broader geographic context remain legible after interaction.

## Visitors always know what changed

Community filter, search results, initiative detail navigation, Impact synthesis refresh and Workspace boundary crossing **explicitly signal change** — not silent substitution.

## Visitors always know where to continue

Exploration links, header destinations, initiative cards, Find Your Community results, Registration Gateway and Workspace continuation visible from outcomes.

## Visitors always know how to return

Region ascent, Country ascent, World ascent, prior community view, search reset, breadcrumb or navigator ascent, header Home — **reversible paths preserved**.

## Navigation follows curiosity

No forced linear tutorial, locked community content or registration wall on public Evidence.

Forbidden at community scope:

- interruptive overlay before Context Introduction read path;
- community guilt framing on exit;
- bait-and-switch from public reading to operational Workspace without explicit governed entry;
- Find Your Community masquerading as mandatory gate;
- organizational join pressure tied to Community Identity image or Description.

# 9. Context Before Evidence

Every interaction preserves the pattern:

```
Heading

↓

Context Introduction

↓

Evidence

↓

Visitor Conclusion
```

| Layer                    | Interaction role at community scope                              |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Heading**              | Visitor identifies community interaction subject                 |
| **Context Introduction** | Visitor understands why community Evidence below matters         |
| **Evidence**             | Visitor receives community-filtered public facts and projections |
| **Visitor Conclusion**   | Visitor judges — platform does not conclude for them             |

**Community Experience explains before presenting evidence.**

Interactions that reveal new Evidence — search result selection, initiative detail navigation, Impact synthesis expand — must preserve **Context Introduction before new Evidence layer**.

**Visitors always form their own conclusions.**

**Humanity Union presents evidence.**

**Visitors form conclusions independently.**

Interactions must not insert platform verdicts, community certification copy or "trust this community" messaging at conclusion layer.

Community Impact Overview **requires** Visitor Conclusion interaction — platform explicitly declines subjective evaluation.

Find Your Community Context Introduction must clarify **participant-created search** before search Evidence.

Registration Gateway and Workspace continuation are **invitation** — not platform conclusion about visitor judgment.

# 10. Trust Through Interaction

Community Experience builds trust through **interaction design discipline** — not through persuasion.

**Trust is earned through observable civic contribution.**

Trust grows when visitors can **verify** what they observe.

## Observable community activity

Statistics and pipeline interactions lead to **traceable public records** — not claims.

## Verified initiatives

Initiative card interactions open **public projection detail** — civic path readable without account.

## Public achievements

Impact Overview interactions expose **derived labeled aggregates** where projection permits — visitor can verify upstream Evidence.

## Transparent participation

Pipeline interactions use **frozen civic vocabulary** — same stages as all upstream scopes; community filter only.

## Consistent navigation

Scope changes, empty states, search honesty, Workspace boundary and return paths are **explainable** — no hidden community or conversion funnels.

Trust grows through:

- observable community activity;
- verified initiatives;
- public achievements where projection permits;
- transparent participation structure;
- consistent navigation.

Community Experience must not ask visitors to trust through:

- organizational rhetoric or community boosterism;
- representative image authority;
- urgency or moral pressure;
- obscured sparse data;
- fabricated community vibrancy;
- platform subjective impact scoring.

**Trust Through Verification** applies at **community scope** identically to Region, Country and World scope.

# 12. Relationship with Workspace

**Community Experience is the final public interaction layer.**

**Workspace is the first personal interaction layer.**

**The transition is intentional and explicit.**

| Community Experience                             | Workspace                                       |
| ------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Public observation interactions                  | Personal operational interactions               |
| Read without account                             | Governed authenticated environment              |
| Projection-backed Evidence blocks                | Capability 02 workspace operations              |
| Registration Gateway / Continue to Workspace     | Workspace UI — outside Public Space composition |
| Every interaction increases public understanding | Interactions enable accountable personal action |

## Transition rules

| Rule                           | Application                                                                        |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Intentional**                | Workspace entry interaction explicitly labeled — environment change visible        |
| **Explicit**                   | Context Introduction explains personal participation before action                 |
| **After observation**          | Evidence interactions precede Workspace Transition on community observation pages  |
| **Voluntary**                  | Visitor may stop at any public interaction stage                                   |
| **No leakage**                 | Workspace operational interactions do not appear inside Community Evidence blocks  |
| **Reversible where permitted** | Return to public Community Experience from governed entry — architecture dependent |

Community Experience interactions **do not**:

- preview Workspace internals as public Evidence substitute;
- require Workspace entry to read initiatives or community statistics;
- use Workspace urgency patterns absent from upstream public scopes.

Workspace interactions **do not**:

- replace Community public observation;
- inherit Community block order inside operational environment;
- re-teach Public Space navigation as different product.

The boundary is **architectural and interaction-explicit** — Public Space ends; personal participation begins.

# 13. Future Evolution

Future Community Experience capabilities may extend exploration **without redesigning Version 1 interaction architecture**.

| Future addition             | Integration rule                                                                                     |
| --------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Community collections**   | Optional visitor or curated grouping — must not gate public Evidence                                 |
| **Saved communities**       | Visitor convenience — must not replace Find Your Community honesty or force account for reading      |
| **Community subscriptions** | Optional notification preference — must not pressure registration or obscure public observation path |
| **Community collaboration** | Cross-community public initiative association interactions — Architecture Review required            |
| **AI community discovery**  | Explain-only assistant — never decides, registers, certifies or ranks communities opaquely           |

**Must preserve the interaction architecture:**

- canonical learning path;
- Context Before Evidence on all blocks;
- Find Your Community principles;
- Every interaction increases understanding;
- No dead ends;
- Observation precedes participation;
- Filter Instead of Duplicate;
- Communities discovered through participation;
- explicit Workspace boundary;
- Region ascent preserved.

New interaction types require **Architecture Review** and Block Library entry — not Epic 05 implementation convenience.

Future capabilities **extend exploration and personal convenience** — they do not **replace** Community Experience interaction model or introduce registration walls on public Evidence.

AI community discovery must:

- explain search and association concepts — not substitute visitor judgment;
- never certify community legitimacy;
- never optimize engagement without explainable honesty;
- preserve reversibility and calm tone.

# 15. Final Statement

**Community Experience completes Humanity Union's Public Experience by transforming public understanding into informed personal participation without changing the unified architectural language.**

Visitors understand one participant-created community through **meaningful interactions** — voluntarily, predictably, reversibly and calmly — before any Workspace invitation.

**It preserves Humanity Union's unified interaction architecture without introducing a new interaction language.**

**The interaction architecture remains stable** while **civic understanding becomes progressively more participant-local**.

Community Experience does not teach visitors a new platform.

It teaches them **more about one community's observable civic life** — and **how personal participation may continue in Workspace when they choose** — inside the platform they already learned at Region, Country and World scope.

Downstream artifacts — page template standard, alignment documents, architecture review, architecture freeze and implementation plan — must conform to this document and frozen Epic 01 through Epic 04 interaction discipline.

**This document does not define UI implementation.**

**This document does not define animations.**

It defines **how Community Experience interactions must behave** — before engineering serves that behaviour.

One Humanity.

Many Countries.

Many Regions.

Many Communities.

Shared Future.

One interaction model — observed globally, understood nationally, explored regionally, **completed communally**, continued personally in Workspace.
