# Purpose

Define the vision of Humanity Union's **Global Public Space**.

This document bridges **Public Information Architecture** (Epic 01 — Information Space) and the future implementation of the **Global Experience** (Epic 02).

The Global Experience is the **first public civic space** encountered by most visitors.

It is the World-scoped entry to Humanity Union — the default public square before Country or Region filtering.

It introduces Humanity Union through **observation rather than persuasion**.

Visitors arrive to see civic life on the platform — not to be sold participation, urgency or platform mythology.

The Global Experience orients society calmly:

- what Humanity Union is;
- what civic activity exists globally;
- how participation is structured and visible;
- how trust may be evaluated;
- how exploration continues without account requirement.

This is a vision document.

It does not define implementation.

It does not authorize visual design, routing or code.

It records architectural intent for the Global Experience before engineering begins.

# Vision

The Global Experience is the **public square of Humanity Union**.

A public square is not a sales funnel.

It is a place where society may:

- **observe** civic activity in progress;
- **understand** how the platform organizes participation;
- **decide for themselves** whether and when to engage further.

People arrive without obligation.

They may browse globally, drill into countries and regions, read initiative public projections, verify derived civic state and leave — successfully — without registering.

The Global Experience presents Humanity Union as:

- one unified civic platform;
- alive with real participation activity;
- transparent about what is recorded and what is derived;
- open to exploration at World scope;
- respectful of visitor autonomy.

It is World-scoped Home in the information architecture — but its **civic meaning** is larger than a conventional homepage.

It is the first impression of Humanity Union as a public institution — not as a product landing page.

Capability 02 Participation feeds live civic content into this square through public projections.

The Global Experience presents that content — it does not replace aggregate authority or operational workspaces.

---

# First Impression

Within seconds of arrival, the Global Experience must communicate:

| Message                   | Meaning for the visitor                                                                                                                       |
| ------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Humanity Union exists** | A coherent civic platform with purpose — not an empty template or vague community site                                                        |
| **Humanity is active**    | Real initiatives and civic activity are visible — not placeholder marketing                                                                   |
| **Participation is real** | Structured participation pipeline activity exists in public view — proposals, decisions, support, commitment, implementation where applicable |
| **Progress is visible**   | Collective civic advancement is observable — derived indicators labeled honestly                                                              |
| **Trust can be verified** | Public information is explainable — the platform does not ask for blind faith                                                                 |

First impression is **orientation and evidence** — not conversion.

The visitor should feel invited to look — not pressured to sign up.

Calm composition, clear hierarchy and honest data beat animation, urgency and superlatives.

First impression must work for all Epic 01 personas:

- **Explorer** — something is happening worth browsing;
- **Researcher** — credible entry into live civic records;
- **Learner** — platform structure becomes intelligible quickly;
- **Verification Seeker** — claims are bounded and inspectable;
- **Future Participant** — participation appears real before registration is offered.

# Visitor Questions

The Global Experience should **naturally answer** the following questions — through block composition and navigation — without forcing a linear tutorial.

## What is Humanity Union?

Answered by: **Hero**, platform framing copy, header navigation context, **About** entry path.

Humanity Union is a civic participation platform — not social media, not project management, not engagement gaming.

## What is happening now?

Answered by: **Latest Global Initiatives**, **Global Statistics**, **Initiative Levels**.

Live and recent civic activity at World scope — honest empty states if activity is sparse in a region of the dataset.

## How is the world participating?

Answered by: **Interactive World Map**, **Initiative Levels**, **Global Statistics**, initiative preview cards.

Geographic distribution and pipeline stage presence show global participation patterns — filtered views lead to Country and Region without architecture change.

## Can I explore?

Answered by: header primary navigation, **Interactive World Map** scope exploration, initiative preview entry, footer and Related Content paths.

Exploration requires no account.

Registration is not implied by scrolling.

## Why should I trust this platform?

Answered by: derived-value labeling, **Global Statistics** honesty, traceable initiative previews, **About** and Knowledge paths, calm absence of certification overclaim.

Trust is earned through verifiable public information — not slogans.

Visitor questions map to experience blocks — not to interruptive modals, pop-ups or forced onboarding flows.

# Public Experience Flow

The Global Experience follows a public flow distinct from operational participation — but aligned with Epic 01 user journey principles.

```
Identity

↓

Orientation

↓

Understanding

↓

Participation
```

This flow describes **experiential sequencing on the Global Experience** — not domain commands or aggregate lifecycles.

## Identity

**Responsibility:** Establish what Humanity Union is as a public civic institution.

The visitor learns the platform exists, serves civic participation and operates in public view.

Identity is conveyed through Hero framing, design tone and calm platform language — not brand hype.

Identity does not require registration.

## Orientation

**Responsibility:** Help the visitor understand where they are and what they can explore.

Orientation covers World scope context, geographic navigation entry, primary header destinations and first-scan block hierarchy.

The visitor knows they are in the global public square and may move to Initiatives, Institutions, Media, Knowledge or About.

Orientation is not completeness — it is **legibility**.

## Understanding

**Responsibility:** Deepen comprehension of live civic activity and platform structure.

Understanding emerges through Statistics, Initiative Levels, map exploration, initiative previews and cross-links to detail and Knowledge.

Derived civic indicators are labeled.

Evidence and progress language remain honest.

Understanding precedes trust-based participation decisions.

## Participation

**Responsibility:** Offer explicit paths toward registration and operational civic action — only after sufficient public comprehension.

Participation entry appears through **Join Humanity Union** block and navigation to initiative detail — never as ambient pressure on first screen.

Participation means informed choice — not default outcome of visiting.

Public Experience flow ends at the threshold of Registration Gateway.

Operational Participation workspaces belong to Capability 02 beyond that threshold.

# Required Experience Blocks

The Global Experience composes **required experience blocks in order** on the shared page structure defined in Epic 01.

Block order follows progressive disclosure: identity and orientation first, understanding next, participation entry last.

These blocks reuse the Public Experience Block Library — Global Experience does not invent parallel block architectures.

## 1. Hero

**Purpose:** Calm platform identity and Global Experience orientation.

The Hero answers: **What is this place?**

It states Humanity Union's civic purpose at World scope in restrained language.

It is not a marketing billboard, video hero or conversion banner.

It frames observation — not urgency.

## 2. Interactive World Map

**Purpose:** Geographic orientation and scope exploration entry.

The Interactive World Map answers: **Where is civic activity happening?**

It helps visitors understand global distribution and navigate toward Country or Region filtered views.

Map interaction changes dataset scope — not page architecture.

The map is orientation — not an operational dashboard.

## 3. Global Statistics

**Purpose:** Aggregate public metrics at World scope.

Global Statistics answers: **How much civic activity exists globally?**

Metrics summarize initiatives, stages and public indicators suitable for global display.

All derived values must be labeled derived.

No gamification, ranking pressure or unverifiable totals.

Statistics create grounds for trust — not promotional impressiveness.

## 4. Initiative Levels

**Purpose:** Pipeline stage distribution at a glance.

Initiative Levels answers: **What kinds of participation are underway?**

It shows how civic activity distributes across Participation pipeline stages in public view.

Stage labels use Capability 02 civic language.

This block orients — it does not replace initiative detail pages.

## 5. Latest Global Initiatives

**Purpose:** Curated entry into live civic activity.

Latest Global Initiatives answers: **What is happening now — specifically?**

It surfaces initiative summary cards from public projections at World scope.

Cards invite exploration of Initiatives destination and detail pages.

Operational workspace internals remain excluded.

## 6. Join Humanity Union

**Purpose:** Explicit registration entry when the visitor chooses participation.

Join Humanity Union answers: **How do I participate if I am ready?**

This block implements Registration Gateway responsibility — deliberate, secondary, unpressured.

It must not dominate first screen or interrupt orientation.

It appears after identity, orientation and understanding blocks in hierarchy.

Public reading is never gated behind this block.

## 7. Footer

**Purpose:** Supporting navigation and platform obligations.

Footer answers: **Where are legal, accessibility, contact and secondary resources?**

Footer carries supporting links — not primary discovery competition.

Footer is global chrome — identical structure across Public Space.

## Block order summary

```
Hero
  → Interactive World Map
    → Global Statistics
      → Initiative Levels
        → Latest Global Initiatives
          → Join Humanity Union
            → Footer (persistent chrome)
```

Header primary navigation and geographic scope selector sit in global chrome above this composition per Navigation Architecture.

# Emotional Tone

The Global Experience must feel:

## Calm

No urgency timers, countdown pressure, moral shaming or engagement bait.

Visitors breathe before they choose depth.

## Respectful

Society is treated as capable of observation and judgment — not as leads to be converted.

Copy respects visitor intelligence and autonomy.

## Open

Exploration is welcome without account requirement.

Empty or sparse global activity is presented honestly — not hidden or exaggerated.

## Hopeful

Civic participation is presented as real, structured and meaningful — without utopian overclaim or cynicism.

Hope emerges from visible activity and honest transparency — not from slogans.

## Never manipulative

Forbidden: dark patterns, false scarcity, social pressure copy, hidden registration requirements, deceptive metrics.

## Never urgent

Forbidden: "Act now," "Don't miss out," "Join before it's too late," interruptive registration modals on arrival.

Emotional tone implements Public Experience Principles — especially **Calm Navigation**, **We create grounds for trust** and **Understanding precedes registration**.

# Design Philosophy

Humanity Union Global Experience should feel:

## Organized

Clear hierarchy, consistent block order, predictable navigation.

Visitors know where to look next without hunting.

## Transparent

Derived values labeled, civic stages named honestly, platform boundaries visible.

Transparency is structural — not a disclaimer buried in footer alone.

## Alive

Real initiatives, real pipeline activity, real geographic presence — not static marketing shell.

Alive does not mean chaotic or noisy.

## International

World scope first — civic activity spans geographies without privileging one nation as default product identity unless dataset honestly reflects concentration.

Country and Region filtering available without leaving unified architecture.

## Accessible

Hierarchy preserved across form factors.

Public information reachable without registration.

Accessibility treated as architectural requirement — not post-hoc fix.

Design philosophy guides visual implementation later.

It does not prescribe colors, typography or framework choices here.

Interface implements philosophy — it does not redefine civic meaning.

# Future Evolution

The Global Experience must **support future capabilities without redesign**.

## Extension through blocks

New public capabilities attach by:

- adding blocks to the library;
- extending Statistics or Initiative Levels datasets;
- enriching Latest Global Initiatives cards;
- adding Related Content cross-links;

—not by replacing Global Experience page architecture.

## Geographic evolution

Future geographic levels attach as map and filter extensions — same block order, same navigation.

## Capability integration

Future Participation stages — Impact public projections, additional pipeline visibility — extend widget data and previews.

They must not require a new Global homepage architecture.

Future Media, Institutions and Knowledge prominence may adjust block emphasis within shared layout — not fork World entry into separate products.

## Reserved expansion

Potential future Global Experience enrichments — **not authorized here**:

- Impact summary block at global scope;
- public observatory or aggregate transparency dashboard;
- featured institutional presence block;
- localized default scope by visitor preference.

Reserved concepts may be named in architecture freeze.

They must not be partially implemented before need and review.

Future evolution follows **Future Extension Without Redesign** from Public Experience Principles.

# Final Statement

**The Global Experience is not a homepage.**

It is **Humanity Union's public civic square**.

It is where society first observes civic life on the platform — globally, calmly and without pressure.

It introduces Humanity Union through evidence and orientation — not persuasion.

It composes required experience blocks on Epic 01 architecture.

It honors visitor questions, emotional tone and design philosophy as architectural requirements.

It leads to exploration, understanding and informed participation — never to manipulation or urgency.

Implementation of Epic 02 must conform to this vision and all Epic 01 foundation documents.

Visual design, routing and engineering follow discovery — they do not replace it.

The Global Experience is the World-scoped front door of civic legitimacy for Humanity Union.

It must be worthy of that responsibility.

This document does not define implementation.
