# 1. Purpose

Define the **interaction architecture** of Region Experience.

This document explains how visitors **progressively understand one region's civic activity** while **naturally discovering communities** through Humanity Union Public Space.

Region Experience **guides visitors from understanding a region toward discovering communities created through civic participation** — not through directed funnels, administrative pressure or registration optimization.

**Every interaction increases understanding.**

Interactions are architectural commitments about:

- what the visitor may do at region scope;
- what changes when they do it;
- what they learn from the change;
- how they continue toward Community Experience, ascend or return.

This document does not define UI implementation, animations, frontend behaviour, components or visual design.

# 2. Interaction Philosophy

Region Experience interactions inherit Global and Country Experience philosophy without regional exception.

Interactions should be:

| Quality         | Architectural meaning at region scope                                                               |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Voluntary**   | Visitor chooses to act — no forced regional paths, gates or interruptive demands                    |
| **Predictable** | Same interaction class produces same outcome — World, Country, Region and Community share behaviour |
| **Reversible**  | Visitor returns to Country, World, prior region view or community without penalty                   |
| **Explainable** | Visitor understands scope change, regional and country context before or immediately after acting   |
| **Respectful**  | Interactions honour attention, dignity and autonomy — no regional boosterism or dark patterns       |
| **Calm**        | No urgency, streaks, countdowns or moral pressure tied to region identity                           |

**Every interaction reduces uncertainty rather than creating it.**

After interacting, the visitor should know:

- where they are — region and country named, Humanity Union context preserved;
- what changed — scope filter, community entry, detail navigation or Evidence refresh;
- what they learned — regional or community civic meaning;
- how to continue — initiatives, communities, header destinations;
- how to go back — Country ascent, World ascent, browser back, navigator.

Forbidden interaction philosophy at region scope:

- regional registration conversion optimization;
- surprise scope changes that feel like leaving Humanity Union;
- hiding regional public Evidence until account creation;
- interactions that rank or shame regions;
- decorative clicks without learning outcome;
- Find Your Community search behaviour on Region page;
- collapsing Region scope into Community scope without explicit transition.

Interactions serve **regional public understanding and community discovery within national and global architecture** — not platform metrics alone.

# 3. Learning Path

The canonical **exploration sequence** for Region Experience interactions:

```
Observe

↓

Understand the Region

↓

Explore Regional Activity

↓

Discover Communities

↓

Understand Communities

↓

Participate (optional)
```

## Observe

Visitor encounters Region Experience — Region Identity, regional map, first scan.

**Interaction goal:** notice **which region within which country** is active and that regional civic activity is observable in Humanity Union.

No commitment required.

## Understand the Region

Visitor reads regional Context Introductions and Evidence — statistics, pipeline, initiative previews.

**Interaction goal:** grasp **structure and scale** of public civic activity **in this region**.

Comprehension deepens regionally — registration does not.

## Explore Regional Activity

Visitor acts — initiative card links, header destinations, regional map inspection, related public records.

**Interaction goal:** move from regional aggregates to **concrete civic subjects** within the region.

Exploration remains **within region scope** unless visitor deliberately changes scope toward Community or ascends to Country.

## Discover Communities

Visitor encounters **Community Discovery** — communities with public activity associated to the region.

**Interaction goal:** understand that **participant-created communities** exist as **next civic context** — Region is geographic filter; Community is participant-named context.

Discovery is **browse and understand associations** — not administrator catalog search.

## Understand Communities

Visitor selects a community entry or follows map association toward **Community Experience**.

**Interaction goal:** grasp **scope difference** — Region observed; Community scope begins participant-named local observation.

Find Your Community on Community Experience remains available for **broader name search** — Region does not duplicate it.

## Participate (optional)

Visitor chooses Registration Gateway on Region page — or later on Community Experience — optional transition to registration and Workspace.

**Interaction goal:** informed entry to accountable participation **after regional and optional community-oriented observation**.

**The visitor naturally narrows attention from region to community:**

```
Humanity Union (global institution)

↓

Country (national filter)

↓

Region (regional filter within country)

↓

Community (participant-named civic context)

↓

Initiative detail (named civic path)

↓

Optional Workspace (accountable action)
```

Understanding becomes **progressively more local** — not a different product at each step.

Participate is valid.

Stopping at Understand Communities is valid.

Stopping at Understand the Region is valid.

Skipping to Participate without Observe–Understand Communities is architecturally discouraged — not forbidden by account gate, but not designed for.

# 4. Region Experience Flow

End-to-end interaction flow from region entry through optional participation:

```
Visitor

↓

Region Experience

↓

Regional Exploration

↓

Community Discovery

↓

Community Experience

↓

Registration (optional)

↓

Workspace
```

## Visitor

External entry — from Country descent, Regional Exploration on Country page, shared region URL, search or navigator.

**Transition responsibility:** land in **unified public architecture** at Region scope — not operational workspace, not regional portal fork.

## Region Experience

Country-filter narrows to **one region within one country**.

**Transition responsibility:** orient regionally — Region Identity, Context Introduction, regional Evidence sequence; country parent context preserved.

Visitor understands they remain **inside Humanity Union**.

## Regional Exploration

Visitor interacts with regional map, statistics, pipeline and initiatives on Region page.

**Transition responsibility:** deepen **regional** comprehension — aggregates to examples to initiative detail links.

Scope remains **Region** unless visitor explicitly transitions toward Community or ascends to Country.

**Note:** _Regional Exploration_ here names **interaction with regional Evidence blocks** — not the Country Experience block titled Regional Exploration, which prepared entry to Region Experience.

## Community Discovery

Visitor interacts with **Community Discovery block** — community association entries, map highlights, scope transition guidance.

**Transition responsibility:** prepare **Community scope** — explain Region ≠ Community; surface participant-created communities with public regional association; link forward to Community Experience.

Does **not** implement Find Your Community search.

## Community Experience

Visitor enters **community-scoped public observation** — when Community Experience exists.

**Transition responsibility:** apply **community filter** — Identity and Evidence refresh at Community scope; Find Your Community available as primary discovery extension.

Same template discipline — participant-named local context.

## Registration (optional)

Visitor chooses Registration Gateway on Region page or Community Experience — calm invitation.

**Transition responsibility:** offer **informed** Identity entry — never required for reading.

## Workspace

Governed operational participation — Capability 02 workspaces.

**Transition responsibility:** accountable civic action **after** public understanding.

Region Experience interaction flow **extends** Country Experience flow — it does not replace it.

Ascent to **Country Experience** and **Global Experience** remains available at every stage.

Deep link directly to Community Experience is valid — template preserved — not architecture failure.

# 5. Geographic Exploration

Geographic exploration at and below Region scope follows frozen hierarchy:

```
Region

↓

Community

↓

Workspace
```

Above Region, **Country** and **World** remain ascent targets:

```
World

↓

Country

↓

Region   ← this Epic

↓

Community

↓

Workspace
```

## Scope distinction

| Scope         | Nature                                          | Interaction rule                                                           |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Region**    | Geographic/administrative filter within country | Region remains **geographic** — map, statistics, pipeline at region filter |
| **Community** | Participant-created civic context               | Community is **participant-created** — not administrator catalog alone     |
| **Workspace** | Personal operational environment                | Governed entry after optional registration                                 |

Region interactions must **never impersonate Community scope**.

Community interactions must **preserve Region ascent**.

## Interaction model

| Transition                       | What changes                                                                              | What does not change                                                    |
| -------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Country → Region**             | Dataset scope; Identity framing; statistics and initiatives filtered regionally           | Page template; block types; header destinations; interaction philosophy |
| **Region → Community**           | Participant-named civic context filter; Community blocks activate on Community Experience | Architecture language; primary navigation; interaction philosophy       |
| **Community → Region**           | Regional geographic filter restore                                                        | Template and navigation                                                 |
| **Region → Country**             | National filter restore                                                                   | Unified Public Space model                                              |
| **Region → World**               | Global filter restore via Country or navigator                                            | Unified Public Space model                                              |
| **Any public scope → Workspace** | Environment shift to operational participation                                            | Projection discipline on public pages                                   |

**Only the geographic or civic context scope changes.**

**Architecture remains identical.**

## Primary geographic interactions at Region scope

- **Geographic Navigator** — explicit Region active within Country; Community routing where supported; Country and World ascent;
- **Regional Interactive Map** — community association highlights; geographic orientation within region;
- **Scoped URLs or state** — deep link to Region or Community Geographic Experience;
- **Community Discovery block** — explains and supports Community transition honestly.

## Reversibility

Visitor returns to Country or World without losing navigation context.

Scope change must signal **what changed** — region label, country label, community name, scope parameter, filtered Evidence refresh.

Forbidden: scope transition that reloads different header set, hides primary destinations or feels like exiting Humanity Union.

# 6. Information Exploration

Information exploration **narrows geographic and civic context while increasing understanding**.

```
Region Overview

↓

Regional Statistics

↓

Participation Pipeline

↓

Regional Initiatives

↓

Community Discovery

↓

Community Experience

↓

Participation
```

## Region Overview

**Interaction locus:** Region Identity · first scan · Geographic Navigator confirmation.

**Understanding gain:** _Where am I regionally within this country in Humanity Union?_

## Regional Statistics

**Interaction locus:** Regional Statistics block; optional expand or calm cross-link.

**Understanding gain:** _How much public civic activity is visible in this region?_

## Participation Pipeline

**Interaction locus:** Regional Participation Pipeline block.

**Understanding gain:** _How is participation distributed across stages regionally?_

## Regional Initiatives

**Interaction locus:** Latest Regional Initiatives cards; links to public initiative detail.

**Understanding gain:** _What concrete civic paths exist in this region?_

## Community Discovery

**Interaction locus:** Community Discovery block; map community highlights; links to Community Experience.

**Understanding gain:** _Which participant-created communities have public activity associated here — and how is Community scope different from Region?_

## Community Experience

**Interaction locus:** Community Identity, Community Statistics, Find Your Community — on Community page when implemented.

**Understanding gain:** _What is happening around this participant-named community?_

## Participation

**Interaction locus:** Registration Gateway on Region or Community Experience.

**Understanding gain:** _What registration enables — chosen action._

Each step is **optional** — visitor may jump via header, deep link or Related Content — but **presentation order** on Region Experience follows this progression.

Narrowing context:

- regional statistics → one initiative → one community association → Community Experience;

Increasing understanding:

- abstract regional counts → named civic path → participant-named community context → verifiable public detail.

**Every interaction increases local relevance while preserving Humanity Union architecture** — same header, same block rhythm, same trust model, same calm scope semantics.

# 7. Community Discovery

**Community Discovery principles are frozen** for Region Experience interactions.

## Community Discovery helps visitors explore

Community Discovery interactions surface **participant-created communities** with **public activity associated to the region** — increasing understanding of local civic context available through participation.

Browsing associations is **exploration** — not mandatory path completion.

## Community Discovery does not require searching

Community Discovery is **not Find Your Community**.

No search field, query box or name-matching interaction belongs on Region page as primary Community Discovery behaviour.

Search-based community discovery belongs to **Community Experience** — participant-driven, voluntary.

## Community Discovery does not behave as an administrator-maintained catalogue

Displayed communities reflect **public projection associations** from civic participation — not an exhaustive administrator-defined directory presented as sole truth.

Empty or sparse association states must be **honest** — no fabricated community lists.

## Community Discovery shows communities created through participation

Communities appear because **Initiative creation and civic activity** introduced or named them — per `COMMUNITY_CONTEXT_DECISION.md`.

Community Discovery **documents observable associations** — it does not **create** Community business truth.

## Community Discovery naturally leads into Community Experience

| Interaction outcome                     | Architectural meaning                                                                 |
| --------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Community entry selected**            | Transition to Community Experience at community scope — Identity and Evidence refresh |
| **Association understood but no entry** | Visitor learned Region vs Community distinction — valid stop                          |
| **Cross-link to Find Your Community**   | Secondary path on Community Experience — not Region search duplicate                  |
| **Return to regional Evidence**         | Reversible — no penalty                                                               |

Community Discovery interactions must:

- signal **what changed** when moving toward Community scope;
- preserve **explainability** — participant-named context named explicitly;
- avoid **dead ends** — honest empty state with Country return and header continue paths;
- never **force** community selection before public reading elsewhere.

# 8. Interaction Principles

The following principles are **frozen** for Region Experience and connected public interactions.

## Every interaction increases understanding

If an interaction cannot articulate what the visitor learned about **this region's public civic activity** or **community association context**, it should not exist.

## No dead ends

Every interaction outcome offers **continue, return or cross-link** — not blank terminus without orientation.

Region scope unavailable, sparse data, empty community associations or deferred Community Experience routes must explain honestly — with Country return path.

## Visitors always know where they are

Region name, country name, scope label, navigator state and Identity layer remain legible after interaction.

## Visitors always know what changed

Scope filter, community entry, initiative detail navigation and Evidence refresh **explicitly signal change** — not silent substitution.

## Visitors always know where to continue

Exploration links, header destinations, initiative cards, community entries and Related Content visible from outcomes.

## Visitors always know how to return

Country ascent, World ascent, Region reset, breadcrumb or navigator ascent, header Home — **reversible paths preserved**.

## Navigation follows curiosity

No forced linear tutorial, locked regional content or registration wall on public Evidence.

Forbidden at region scope:

- interruptive overlay before Context Introduction read path;
- regional guilt framing on exit;
- bait-and-switch from public reading to operational workspace without explicit governed entry;
- Community search masquerading as Community Discovery.

# 9. Context Before Evidence

Every interaction preserves the pattern:

```
Heading

↓

Context Introduction

↓

Evidence

↓

Visitor Conclusion
```

| Layer                    | Interaction role at region scope                              |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Heading**              | Visitor identifies regional interaction subject               |
| **Context Introduction** | Visitor understands why regional Evidence below matters       |
| **Evidence**             | Visitor receives region-filtered public facts and projections |
| **Visitor Conclusion**   | Visitor judges — platform does not conclude for them          |

Interactions that reveal new Evidence — community entry, initiative detail navigation, map expand — must preserve **Context Introduction before new Evidence layer**.

**Visitors always form their own conclusions.**

**Humanity Union presents evidence.**

**Visitors form conclusions independently.**

Interactions must not insert platform verdicts, regional certification copy or "trust this region" messaging at conclusion layer.

Community Discovery Context Introduction must clarify **Region vs Community** before association Evidence.

Registration Gateway is **invitation** — not platform conclusion about visitor judgment.

---

# 10. Trust Through Interaction

Region Experience builds trust through **interaction design discipline** — not through persuasion.

**Trust is earned through evidence.**

Trust grows when visitors can **verify** what they observe.

## Observable civic activity

Regional map, initiatives and pipeline interactions lead to **traceable public records** — not claims.

## Verified public statistics

Statistics interactions expose **derived labeling** and scope — visitor can question aggregates honestly presented at region filter.

## Regional initiatives

Initiative card interactions open **public projection detail** — civic path readable without account.

## Community participation

Community Discovery interactions surface **public-safe community associations** — participant-created names and summary indicators traceable to Community Experience — not authority claims by Humanity Union about community legitimacy.

## Transparent navigation

Scope changes, empty community associations, placeholder states and return paths are **explainable** — no hidden regional or community funnels.

**Trust grows through understanding rather than persuasion.**

Region Experience must not ask visitors to trust through:

- regional rhetoric or administrative boosterism;
- representative image authority;
- urgency or moral pressure;
- obscured sparse data;
- fabricated community vibrancy.

Trust Through Verification applies at **region scope** identically to Country and World scope.

Region Experience **inherits Country Experience interaction architecture**.

Epic 03 Country Experience is the **direct parent interaction reference**.

| Inherited interaction element | Region behaviour                                                                                                             |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Learning path shape           | Observe → Understand → Explore → Discover Communities → Understand Communities → Participate — regional and community nuance |
| Interaction philosophy        | Voluntary, predictable, reversible, explainable, respectful, calm                                                            |
| Header navigation             | Same six destinations — same interaction outcomes                                                                            |
| Geographic Navigator pattern  | Scope control — Region active within Country                                                                                 |
| Map interaction class         | Scope exploration and community association highlights — regional instead of national                                        |
| Initiative detail entry       | Same public projection pages                                                                                                 |
| Registration Gateway ethics   | After Evidence — optional                                                                                                    |
| Context Before Evidence       | Mandatory on all blocks                                                                                                      |
| Reversibility                 | Country and World ascent always available                                                                                    |

**Only geographic scope changes.**

Country datasets become region-filtered datasets.

Country Identity interaction pattern becomes region Identity — with country parent context.

Country Regional Exploration **prepared** Region entry; Region Experience **completes** regional observation.

**Interaction model, trust model, and architectural language remain identical.**

**Visitors should never need to relearn navigation** when descending from Country Experience to Region Experience.

Interaction muscle memory transfers:

- same header;
- same block rhythm;
- same calm scope-change semantics;
- same trust verification path.

Region is **narrower context** — not **new interaction language**.

# 12. Relationship with Community Experience

**Community Experience continues the interaction model.**

Region Experience **prepares**; Community Experience **completes** local public observation.

| Region role                              | Community continuation                            |
| ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Regional orientation complete            | Community filter applied on Community Experience  |
| Community Discovery exposes associations | Find Your Community extends discovery by name     |
| Scope clarity interaction                | Community Identity replaces Region Identity label |
| Reversible Region ascent                 | Community ↔ Region ↔ Country ↔ World preserved    |

**Visitors never relearn navigation** when moving from Region Experience to Community Experience.

Community Experience interactions do not introduce:

- new header destinations;
- new registration pressure patterns;
- new block order at template level;
- new trust model.

Region → Community transition interaction must:

- signal **what changed** — community name, participant-named context, finer civic filter;
- preserve **explainability** — same template language;
- avoid **dead ends** — honest stub if Community Experience not yet implemented, with Region return.

Find Your Community search interactions live on **Community Experience** — Region Community Discovery **links forward**, not **duplicates**.

Community Experience inherits **same interaction classes** — map absent in Version 1 Community composition per Community Context Decision; ascent to Region preserved.

# 13. Future Evolution

Future **local administrative levels** integrate through **Community Experience** — not by multiplying Region interaction models.

| Future addition                                                 | Integration rule                                                                     |
| --------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Community Experience full epic**                              | Inherits Region handoff; Find Your Community primary search                          |
| **District, Municipality, City, Village, Indigenous Territory** | Civic context through Community or Architecture Review — not Region interaction fork |
| **Advanced Search**                                             | Exploration extension — does not replace block sequence                              |
| **Bookmarks**                                                   | Visitor convenience — must not gate public Evidence                                  |
| **AI Discovery Assistant**                                      | Explain-only — never decides, registers or certifies regional or community activity  |
| **Personal Collections**                                        | Optional visitor organization — must not gate public reading                         |
| **Trusted Regional Media**                                      | Optional supporting interaction — if authorized; same omission semantics             |

**Interaction architecture remains unchanged.**

Extension rules:

- canonical learning path preserved;
- Context Before Evidence preserved;
- Community Discovery principles preserved;
- Every interaction increases understanding preserved;
- No dead ends preserved;
- Observation precedes participation preserved;
- Filter Instead of Duplicate preserved;
- Communities discovered through participation preserved.

New interaction types require **Architecture Review** and Block Library entry — not Epic 04 implementation convenience.

Future capabilities **extend exploration** — they do not **replace** Region Experience interaction model.

# 14. Architectural Principles Confirmed

Region Experience interaction architecture confirms frozen Epic 01, Epic 02 and Epic 03 principles:

| Principle                                            | Region Experience application                                                  |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Observation precedes participation**               | Registration Gateway follows regional Evidence and Community Discovery weight  |
| **Context Before Evidence**                          | Mandatory on every region block interaction                                    |
| **Every interaction increases understanding**        | Regional and community scope changes must teach — not confuse                  |
| **Trust Through Verification**                       | Initiative detail, derived labels, public projections traceable                |
| **Explainable Honesty**                              | Sparse regional data and empty community associations honest — no fabrication  |
| **One Experience Block — One Responsibility**        | Interactions map to single block purpose — no regional megablocks              |
| **Communities are discovered through participation** | Community Discovery surfaces associations — does not admin-catalog communities |

Additional binding principles:

| Principle                                            | Application                                                                 |
| ---------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Public Space is the window into a living society** | Regional Evidence presents observable civic activity in one region          |
| **Navigation serves intentions**                     | Header and footer support exploration — not implementation exposure         |
| **Filter Instead of Duplicate**                      | Region interactions reuse Country and Global classes — scope parameter only |

Principles govern interaction ethics.

Content architecture governs block meaning.

Template governs sequence.

Region scope governs **filter** — not **exception**.

Community scope governs **participant-named context** — not **Region replacement**.

# 15. Final Statement

**Region Experience helps visitors understand one region while naturally discovering communities that emerge through civic participation.**

Visitors explore regionally and toward communities through **meaningful interactions** — voluntarily, predictably, reversibly and calmly.

**It preserves Humanity Union's unified interaction architecture without introducing a new interaction language.**

**The interaction architecture remains stable** while **geographic and civic understanding becomes progressively more local**.

Region Experience does not teach visitors a new platform.

It teaches them **more about one regional place** — and **how participant-created communities connect to it** — inside the platform they already learned at Country and World scope.

Downstream artifacts — page template standard, alignment documents, architecture review, architecture freeze and implementation plan — must conform to this interaction architecture and frozen Epic 01, Epic 02 and Epic 03 documents.

**This document does not define UI implementation.**

**This document does not define animations.**

It defines **how Region Experience interactions must behave** architecturally — before engineering interprets them.

One Humanity.

Many Countries.

Many Regions.

Many Communities.

Shared Future.

One interaction model — observed globally, understood nationally, explored regionally, completed communally.
