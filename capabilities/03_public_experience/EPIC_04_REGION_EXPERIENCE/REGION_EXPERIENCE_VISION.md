# Purpose

Define the **architectural vision** of **Region Experience** within Humanity Union Public Space.

Region Experience presents the **civic activity of one administrative region** while preserving Humanity Union's **unified Public Space architecture**.

It is Geographic Experience at **Region scope** — the regional expression of the same public square Epic 03 established at Country scope and Epic 02 at World scope.

This document records **architectural intent** before content architecture, interaction architecture, specification and implementation.

It does not define implementation.

It does not authorize visual design, routing, components or Capability 02 engineering.

# 1. Vision

Region Experience allows visitors to **understand one region's civic activity** within the broader context of its country and Humanity Union.

A visitor who narrows from Country to Region does not leave Humanity Union.

They do not leave the country context that locates the region.

They **focus** — applying the same architectural lens to civic activity associated with one regional place within one nation.

Region Experience strengthens **local awareness** without separating the region from its country or from Humanity Union's global civic space.

The regional view answers a human question:

**"What is happening here — in this region — within structured civic participation?"**

Region Experience is not a regional portal competing with Humanity Union or with its parent country.

It is **one region's window** into the same living society the Country Experience reveals at national scope and Global Experience reveals at World scope.

Visitors may:

- observe regional civic activity in public view;
- understand how participation is distributed within the region;
- see how regional activity relates to national and global civic context;
- explore communities, initiatives and related public records;
- return to Country and World scope without disorientation;
- choose participation voluntarily — never as a condition of reading.

Region Experience presents **filtered public projections** — Capability 02 civic truth at region scope, composed by Capability 03 into the same Experience Blocks visitors already know from Country and Global Experience.

It reveals regional observable civic reality.

It does not promote a region, an administration or a local political narrative.

---

# 2. Purpose

Region Experience exists to:

## Present regional civic participation

Make structured civic activity **visible at region scope** — initiatives, pipeline stages and geographic distribution within regional boundaries as defined by public-safe metadata.

## Strengthen local awareness

Support visitors who seek **place-relevant civic context** at regional depth — residents, local researchers, journalists and curious citizens — without trapping them in isolation from country or global context.

## Prepare visitors for Community Experience

Serve as the **geographic bridge** between national observation and participant-named local civic context — explaining scope before Community descent without implementing Community blocks or search on the Region page.

## Preserve country and global context

Maintain **ascending paths to Country and World scope**, unified header navigation and language that presents the region as a **participating place within one country within one connected civic space** — not a separate platform.

**Region Experience explains rather than promotes.**

It orients regionally.

It never substitutes marketing for evidence.

It never demands registration to read public information.

Purpose aligns with Epic 01 Public Space model:

**Public Space enables observation.**

**Workspace enables participation.**

Region Experience remains firmly in Public Space.

---

# 3. Guiding Principle

**One Humanity.**

**Many Countries.**

**Many Regions.**

**Shared Future.**

Regions remain **distinct** — named, locatable, administratively and culturally real to visitors within their countries.

Regions simultaneously **participate in one connected civic space** — Humanity Union — where structured civic activity may be observed across places without architectural fragmentation.

| Layer              | Meaning                                                                                         |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **One Humanity**   | One Public Space architecture, one trust model, one platform identity                           |
| **Many Countries** | National scope filter — country locates every region                                            |
| **Many Regions**   | Regional scope filter and regional Identity — not separate products                             |
| **Shared Future**  | Global and national perspective preserved — regional view deepens understanding, not separation |

**Regions are distinct but remain part of one connected civic space.**

Geographic scope changes **what data is shown**.

It does not change **what Humanity Union is**.

Region Experience expresses the guiding principle architecturally:

```
Same template

+

Region scope parameter

+

Country parent context

=

Regional civic observation within unified Public Space
```

Forbidden vision:

- regional silos with different navigation;
- region pages that hide Country or World context;
- rhetoric that pits regions against Humanity Union or against their parent country;
- treating administrative region as identical to participant-named Community.

# 4. Visitor Experience

Visitors to Region Experience should understand — naturally, through composition and calm interaction — the following:

## Where they are

**In Humanity Union, observing one region within one country.**

Answered by: Region Identity Hero, Geographic Navigator with Region active within Country, scope labels on Evidence blocks, reversible ascent to Country and World.

## What is happening in this region

**What public civic activity exists at regional scope** — scale, pipeline shape and concrete initiative examples.

Answered by: Regional Statistics, Regional Participation Pipeline, Latest Regional Initiatives, Regional Interactive Map at region filter.

## How this region contributes to the country

**Regional activity is part of national civic life** — not isolated from the country that contains it.

Answered by: Context Introduction copy naming country context, Country ascent in navigator, regional Evidence positioned within national public participation — not competitive regional ranking.

## How to continue toward Community Experience

**Where local civic context deepens next** — participant-named communities discovered through participation, not administrative directory alone.

Answered by: Community Exploration scope transition block, links toward Community contexts where public associations exist, scope clarity that Region ≠ Community, absence of Find Your Community as Region page primary composition.

## How participation remains voluntary

**Registration is optional** — available after sufficient regional context, never required to read.

Answered by: Registration Gateway after Evidence and exploration sequence — same ethics as Country and Global Experience.

Visitor experience preserves Epic 01 journey:

Discover → Understand → Trust → Register → Participate.

Stopping at Understand or Trust is valid.

Region scope **narrows context**.

It does not **accelerate registration**.

It does not **substitute Community scope prematurely**.

# 5. Regional Perspective

Region Experience introduces a **regional perspective** through filtered public representations — not independent systems.

Each element below uses the **same architectural block pattern** as Country Experience.

Only scope, parent country context and Identity copy adapt.

## Region identity

**Architectural role:** Identity layer — Hero and navigator name the region **within its country**; optional restrained regional visual context such as representative landscape where policy permits.

**Vision constraint:** Identity orients — it does not certify administrative authority, regional superiority or territorial claims.

Regional identity answers **where am I within this country** — not **this region is the center of civic life**.

## Regional statistics

**Architectural role:** Statistics block Evidence — Participation Public Statistics Projection filtered to region scope.

**Vision constraint:** derived values labeled; honest zero or low activity; no league tables comparing regions as civic virtue competition.

Regional statistics quantify **observable public activity** — not regional worth or administrative performance scores.

## Regional initiatives

**Architectural role:** Latest Initiatives Evidence — Initiative Public Projection entries associated with the region.

**Vision constraint:** read-only cards linking to public detail; no operational fields; recency or curation transparent if curated.

Regional initiatives show **concrete civic paths at regional depth** — not local promotional campaigns.

## Regional exploration

**Architectural role:** Exploration layer — Regional Interactive Map, Geographic Navigator and Community Exploration block preparing descent to Community Experience within the same architecture.

**Vision constraint:** community transition explains scope change — Region is geographic filter, Community is participant-named context; reversible ascent to Country; Find Your Community remains Community Experience primary entry.

Regional exploration **deepens local understanding** — it does not fork architecture or replace Community Experience.

## Connection to communities

**Architectural role:** scope transition and contextual links — Region page may route toward communities with public activity associated to the region without becoming a Community directory or search surface.

**Vision constraint:** communities are discovered through participation per Community Context Decision; Region prepares — Community Experience completes local observation.

Connection to communities **bridges geographic and participant-named context** — it does not collapse them into one scope.

**All regional perspective elements are filtered public representations** composed into frozen Experience Blocks.

They are not separate regional databases, regional admin consoles or regional product modules exposed in Public Space.

Capability 02 owns civic truth through public projections.

Capability 03 composes regional understanding through the same block catalog visitors already trust at Country and World scope.

# 6. Relationship with Country Experience

Region Experience **inherits Country Experience architecture**.

Epic 03 Country Experience is the **direct reference** for Home-class composition at Country scope.

Region Experience is Home-class composition at **Region scope**.

| Inherited from Country Experience | Unchanged                                                                                        |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Canonical block sequence pattern  | Region Identity through Footer                                                                   |
| Global chrome                     | Header, Geographic Navigator, Footer                                                             |
| Page template flow                | Identity → Context Introduction → Evidence → Exploration → Participation → Supporting Navigation |
| Interaction philosophy            | Calm, voluntary, explainable, reversible                                                         |
| Trust model                       | Projection boundary, derived labeling, honest sparsity                                           |
| Registration ethics               | Observation precedes participation                                                               |
| Primary navigation                | Six frozen header destinations                                                                   |
| Context Before Evidence           | Heading → Context Introduction → Evidence → Visitor Conclusion                                   |

**Only the geographic scope changes.**

Country datasets become region-filtered datasets.

Country Identity copy becomes region Identity copy — always within named country.

National map Evidence pattern becomes regional civic Evidence.

**Interaction model, trust model, and architectural language remain identical.**

```
Country Experience

+ region scope parameter

= Region Experience
```

Visitors descend from Country — they do not migrate to a different product.

Country Experience **Regional Exploration** prepared this transition.

Region Experience **completes regional observation** and **prepares Community descent** — it does not re-implement national Evidence.

| Transition             | Visitor understanding                                         |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Country → Region**   | National civic activity narrows to one region                 |
| **Region → Country**   | Ascent restores national context                              |
| **Region → World**     | Ascent via Country or navigator restores global public square |
| **Region → Community** | Geographic filter yields to participant-named civic context   |

# 7. Relationship with Community Experience

Region Experience **prepares visitors to discover participant-created communities**.

Community Experience becomes the **next level of exploration** in the frozen geographic hierarchy.

Per `COMMUNITY_CONTEXT_DECISION.md`:

| Region role                   | Community continuation                                                                                |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Geographic bridge             | Community is participant-named — not administrative geography alone                                   |
| Scope clarity                 | Visitor understands Region ≠ Community before descent                                                 |
| Exploration entry             | Region may route toward Community contexts — Find Your Community remains Community Experience block 1 |
| No Community blocks at Region | Community search, Community Statistics and Find Your Community excluded from Region page composition  |

Region does **not** answer **what is happening around this community**.

Region **prepares** the visitor for that question.

Community Experience will present community-scoped public civic activity — filtered projections associated with participant-created or participant-named Community records.

Communities emerge through **Initiative creation** and **Find Your Community** discovery — not administrator-only catalogs.

Region Experience establishes **regional civic observation** that Community Experience **completes** before optional Workspace participation.

```
Region Experience

↓

Community Experience

↓

Workspace
```

Deep link entry directly to Community Experience is a valid visitor shortcut — it does not invalidate Region architecture.

Region remains the **parent geographic context** for communities locatable within regional public associations.

# 8. Architectural Principles

Region Experience conforms to frozen Epic 01, Epic 02 and Epic 03 principles without exception.

## Public Space is the window into a living society

Region pages present **observable regional civic activity** — not regional branding or administrative boosterism exercises.

## Observation precedes participation

Registration Gateway follows regional Evidence — never opens the region page.

## Context Before Evidence

Every block at region scope: **Heading → Context Introduction → Evidence → Visitor Conclusion**.

Regional Context Introduction explains significance — never persuades.

## Trust Through Verification

Visitors trace regional Evidence to initiative public detail and derived labels — same verification path as Country and Global Experience.

## Explainable Honesty

Regional sparse or zero activity shown honestly — not inflated to appear regionally "active."

## One Experience Block — One Responsibility

Region Experience uses the same block catalog pattern — no regional megablocks merging Identity, Evidence and Registration.

## Filter Instead of Duplicate

Region Experience is **scope filter on unified architecture** — not RegionPageTemplate, not parallel block library, not region-specific navigation logic.

Principles govern tone and honesty.

Template governs sequence.

Scope governs datasets.

Country context governs regional placement.

Region Experience adds **regional focus** — not **regional exception**.

# 9. Success Criteria

Region Experience vision succeeds when a visitor moving from **Country Experience to Region Experience** **immediately recognizes**:

| Recognition                             | Expected outcome                                                                        |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **The same Humanity Union**             | Global chrome, platform identity, connected civic space language                        |
| **The same architectural language**     | Block order pattern, page rhythm, Context Before Evidence                               |
| **The same interaction philosophy**     | Calm scope change, reversible navigation, no pressure                                   |
| **Regional scope naturally understood** | "I am observing this region's civic activity within this country within Humanity Union" |
| **Country context preserved**           | Region locatable within named country; Country ascent obvious                           |

Success within approximately **thirty seconds** at Region scope means:

- the region and its country are identified clearly;
- regional public activity is legible or honestly sparse;
- Country and World return paths are obvious;
- Region vs Community distinction is approachable before Community descent;
- registration is not demanded.

Success includes visitors who:

- ascend to Country and World without relearning navigation;
- explore initiatives regionally without an account;
- proceed toward Community Experience when ready — voluntarily;
- leave successfully without registering.

Failure modes to avoid:

- regional page feels like a different website;
- header or block order changes at region scope;
- regional rhetoric replaces Evidence;
- hidden or broken Country or World ascent;
- Region page impersonates Community scope or hosts Find Your Community.

# 10. Final Statement

**Region Experience is the regional expression of Humanity Union Public Space.**

It preserves **one architecture**, **one philosophy**, and **one trust model** while presenting **one region's observable civic reality**.

Region Experience does not fragment Humanity Union.

It does not fragment the country that contains the region.

It **focuses** Humanity Union — one region at a time — for visitors who need regional civic context within the same public institution Country Experience introduced at national scope and Global Experience introduced at World scope.

Downstream artifacts — content architecture, interaction architecture, narrative, page template standard, alignment documents, architecture review, architecture freeze and implementation plan — must inherit this vision and conform to frozen Epic 01, Epic 02 and Epic 03 documents.

**This document does not define implementation.**

It defines **what Region Experience must mean** architecturally before build authorization.

One Humanity.

Many Countries.

Many Regions.

Shared Future.

One Public Space — observed globally, understood nationally, explored regionally, completed communally.
