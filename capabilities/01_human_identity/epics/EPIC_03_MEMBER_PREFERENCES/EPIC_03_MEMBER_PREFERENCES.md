# EPIC_03_MEMBER_PREFERENCES

## Capability 01 — Human Identity

Humanity Union Platform

Epic Specification

Version 1.0

---

# Purpose

Epic 03 establishes the Member Preferences domain.

Its purpose is to allow every Member to personalize the Humanity Union experience while preserving user ownership of preferences.

Preferences belong to the Member but remain independent from Authentication, Verification, Visibility, and Participation.

---

# Mission

Allow the platform to adapt to the Member instead of forcing the Member to adapt to the platform.

---

# Scope

Epic 03 includes:

* Language Preferences
* Localization Preferences
* Time Zone
* Date & Time Format
* Notification Preferences (foundation)
* Accessibility Preferences (foundation)
* Workspace Preferences (foundation)

Epic 03 does not include:

* Translation Engine
* Email notifications
* Push notifications
* SMS
* Theme switching
* AI personalization
* Privacy controls
* Geo-personalization

Those choices belong to the Member and must always remain under the Member's control.
